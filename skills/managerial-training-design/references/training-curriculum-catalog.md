# Sample Training Curriculum Catalog

## Basic Skills (4-8 hours)
- Telephone Manners
- Email Etiquette
- Time Management
- Basic Business Writing

## Technical Skills (16-200 hours)
- Software Tools Fundamentals (16 hours)
- Equipment Operation Basics (24 hours)
- Ion Implanter Operation (200 hours)
- Quality Control Procedures (40 hours)

## Management Disciplines (8-24 hours)
- Strategic Planning (16 hours)
- Constructive Confrontation (8 hours)
- Performance Reviews (12 hours)
- Conducting Productive Meetings (8 hours)
- Task-Relevant Management (12 hours)

## Leadership Development (24-40 hours)
- Delegation Skills (8 hours)
- Team Building (16 hours)
- Change Management (16 hours)
- Cross-Functional Collaboration (12 hours)

## Recommended Time Allocation
- Classroom learning: 2-4% of employee time
- New employee onboarding: 40-80 hours in first 90 days
- Annual refresher: 8-16 hours per employee
