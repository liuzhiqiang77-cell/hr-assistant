# Manager-Led Training - Detailed Reference

## Variable Definitions

### High-Leverage Training Variables
- **prep_ratio** (numeric): Hours of preparation required per hour of course time (standard: 3)
- **total_student_hours** (numeric): Total annual work hours of the trained group
- **performance_improvement** (percentage): Efficiency gain achieved through training (e.g., 1%)

### Training Volume Variables
- **Annual Turnover Rate** (Percentage): The percentage of staff leaving the organization annually
- **Annual Growth Rate** (Percentage): The percentage the organization grows annually
- **Task 1 Load** (Percentage): The percentage of the staff requiring basic training (Turnover + Growth)
- **Task 2 Magnitude** (Multiplier): The factor by which training all staff exceeds training new staff

## Result Templates

- **High-Leverage Training:** Increased organizational output (measured in hours gained or productivity percentage) and reduced risk of operational failure/costs
- **Training Strategy:** Identification of required trainer, classification of training task (New Member vs. New Skill), and calculated percentage of staff requiring training (e.g., 20% vs. 100%)
