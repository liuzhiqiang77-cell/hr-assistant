# Training Volume Calculation Examples

## Example 1: High-Growth Startup

**Organization Metrics**:
- Current Staff: 100 employees
- Annual Turnover Rate: 20%
- Annual Growth Rate: 50%

**Task 1 Calculation**:
```
Annual Training Load = 20% + 50% = 70%
```
**Implication**: Must train 70% of staff annually (70 employees) just to handle turnover and growth.

**Task 2 Comparison**:
```
Task 2 Magnitude = 100% / 70% = 1.4x
```
**Implication**: Training all 100 employees is only 1.4x larger than new member training. Task 2 is relatively feasible.

## Example 2: Stable Corporation

**Organization Metrics**:
- Current Staff: 1,000 employees
- Annual Turnover Rate: 5%
- Annual Growth Rate: 3%

**Task 1 Calculation**:
```
Annual Training Load = 5% + 3% = 8%
```
**Implication**: Must train 8% of staff annually (80 employees) for new member training.

**Task 2 Comparison**:
```
Task 2 Magnitude = 100% / 8% = 12.5x
```
**Implication**: Training all 1,000 employees is 12.5x larger than new member training. Task 2 is a massive undertaking requiring careful justification.

## Example 3: Cost Analysis

**Scenario**: One-day training course for 50 middle managers

**Cost Calculation**:
- Manager's average hourly rate: $100
- Course duration: 8 hours
- Number of participants: 50

**Total Student Time Cost**:
```
$100/hour × 8 hours × 50 participants = $40,000
```

**Plus Preparation Time**:
- Preparation ratio: 3:1
- Preparation hours: 24 hours
- Trainer hourly rate: $150

**Preparation Cost**:
```
$150/hour × 24 hours = $3,600
```

**Total Direct Cost**: $43,600

**Opportunity Cost** (work not done during training):
```
50 participants × 8 hours = 400 hours of lost productivity
```

**ROI Requirement**:
To justify this investment, the training must generate at least $43,600 in value or 400+ hours of productivity gains.

## Example 4: Training Catalogue Structure

**Intel-Style University Catalogue** (50+ classes):

| Category | Example Courses | Duration |
|----------|----------------|----------|
| Basic Skills | Telephone Manners, Email Etiquette | 2-4 hours |
| Technical Skills | Ion Implanter Operation, Process Control | 40-200 hours |
