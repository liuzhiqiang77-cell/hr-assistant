# Internal Measures: Detailed Examples

## People Development

**Mentoring**:
- Number of mentees and their progress
- Time spent coaching junior team members
- Success stories of mentees promoted or excelling

**Training**:
- Training programs designed or delivered
- Knowledge sharing sessions conducted
- Documentation created for team onboarding

---

## Organizational Health

**Culture Building**:
- Contributing to positive team environment
- Mediating conflicts successfully
- Modeling desired behaviors

**Process Improvement**:
- Workflow optimizations implemented
- Tools or systems adopted that improve efficiency
- Best practices documented and shared

**Risk Mitigation**:
- Identifying and addressing potential issues before they become problems
- Building redundancy or backup systems
- Compliance and governance activities

---

## Relationship Building

**Internal Networks**:
- Cross-functional collaboration effectiveness
- Building alliances with other departments
- Influencing without authority

**External Networks**:
- Industry relationships built
- Partnership development
- Representing organization externally

---

## Strategic Contributions

**Innovation**:
- New ideas proposed (even if not yet implemented)
- Experimentation with new approaches
- Challenging status quo constructively

**Knowledge Management**:
- Lessons learned captured
- Insights shared with broader organization
- Building institutional memory

---

## Assessment Tips

**Look for Patterns**:
- One-time activities less valuable than sustained behaviors
- Consistency in internal contributions matters

**Consider Impact**:
- Not all internal activities have equal value
- Focus on high-leverage contributions

**Verify Quality**:
- Mentoring that produces results vs. just "spending time"
- Process improvements that actually improve efficiency
