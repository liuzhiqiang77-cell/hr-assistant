# Example Training Curriculum

Based on Intel Corporation benchmark (50+ classes)

## Basic Skills
- Telephone manners
- Email etiquette
- Time management
- Basic computer skills

## Technical Skills
- Ion implanter operation (~200 hours)
- Equipment maintenance
- Quality control procedures
- Safety protocols

## Management Disciplines
- Strategic planning
- "Constructive confrontation"
- Performance reviews
- Productive meetings
- Task-relevant maturity
- Performance diagnosis

## Training Volume Calculation Examples

### Example 1: Stable Organization
- Turnover: 5%
- Growth: 2%
- Task 1 Load: 7%
- Task 2 Magnitude: 14.3x
- **Interpretation:** Training all existing staff is 14 times more effort than training new hires

### Example 2: High-Growth Startup
- Turnover: 20%
- Growth: 50%
- Task 1 Load: 70%
- Task 2 Magnitude: 1.4x
- **Interpretation:** With high growth, training existing staff is only slightly more effort than onboarding

### Example 3: Declining Organization
- Turnover: 15%
- Growth: -5%
- Task 1 Load: 10%
- Task 2 Magnitude: 10x
- **Interpretation:** Focus on new hire training; limit new skill training due to shrinking resources
