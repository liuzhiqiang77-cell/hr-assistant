# Interview Question Categories

## Technical/Skills
Focus on assessing the candidate's technical capabilities and skill development:
- Projects highly regarded by management
- Weaknesses and improvement efforts

## What They Did With Knowledge
Focus on past performance and learning from experience:
- Past achievements and their significance
- Past failures and lessons learned
- Problems encountered and solutions implemented

## Discrepancies
Focus on self-awareness and growth:
- How they prevent problems from recurring
- Learning from failures

## Operational Values
Focus on alignment and readiness:
- Readiness for the new job
- Why the company should hire them
- What they value in an employer
