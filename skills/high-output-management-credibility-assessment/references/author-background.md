# Andrew S. Grove Professional Background

## Timeline
- **1956**: Emigrated from Hungary to the United States
- **1968**: Participated in the founding of Intel
- **1979**: Served as President of Intel
- **1987**: Served as Chief Executive Officer
- **1997**: Named Time magazine's Man of the Year
- **1998**: Retired as CEO
- **2004**: Retired as Chairman
- **Academic**: Taught at Stanford University Graduate School of Business for twenty-four years

## Key Relationships
- **Gordon Moore**: Intel co-founder who mentored Grove and recognized his managerial potential
- **Middle Managers**: Collaborators who provided real-world examples and tested Grove's management ideas
