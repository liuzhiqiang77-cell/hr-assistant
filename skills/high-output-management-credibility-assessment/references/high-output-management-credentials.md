# Andrew S. Grove - Professional Background

## Early Career
- Emigrated from Hungary to the United States in 1956
- Participated in the founding of Intel Corporation

## Executive Leadership
- **President of Intel**: Starting in 1979
- **Chief Executive Officer**: Starting in 1987
- **Chairman**: Retired in 2004

## Recognition
- **Time Magazine's Man of the Year**: 1997
- Recognized for leadership during critical periods in technology industry

## Academic Contribution
- **Stanford University Graduate School of Business**: Taught for twenty-four years
- Bridged practical management experience with academic rigor

## Book Development
- **Collaborative Process**: Worked with many Intel managers over years
- **Editorial Partnership**: Grant Ujifusa translated "engineeringese" to accessible English
- **Real-World Testing**: Middle managers provided experiences and served as guinea pigs for early concepts
