## Open-Ended Questions

1. What was the most valuable thing you learned?

2. What was confusing or unclear?

3. What would you like more of?

4. What would you like less of?

5. Any other suggestions for improvement?

## Interpreting Feedback

**Expected Pattern:**
- Some say "too detailed"
- Some say "too superficial"
- Some say "just right"
- This is normal and indicates you're in the right zone

**Red Flags:**
- Everyone says same thing (e.g., all say too fast)
- Low scores across all categories
- No one can apply what they learned

**Success Indicators:**
- Most say they can apply learning immediately
- Specific, actionable suggestions
- Overall positive tone despite critiques
