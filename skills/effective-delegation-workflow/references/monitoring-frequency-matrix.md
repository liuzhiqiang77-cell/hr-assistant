# Monitoring Frequency Matrix

| TRM Level | Task Complexity | Monitoring Frequency | Checkpoint Type |
|-----------|----------------|---------------------|----------------|
| Low | Low | Daily | Brief status check |
| Low | Medium | Every 2-3 days | Review progress, answer questions |
| Low | High | Daily | Detailed review at each stage |
| Medium | Low | Weekly | Status update, spot check |
| Medium | Medium | Twice weekly | Review milestones, provide guidance |
| Medium | High | Every 2-3 days | Review drafts, provide feedback |
| High | Low | Bi-weekly | Final review only |
| High | Medium | Weekly | Check-in at key milestones |
| High | High | Twice weekly | Review at lowest-added-value stages |

## Monitoring Checkpoints

### For Reports/Documents
- Outline review
- Rough draft review
- Final review before distribution

### For Projects
- Kickoff meeting
- Milestone reviews
- Final deliverable review

### For Ongoing Tasks
- Daily/weekly status (based on TRM)
- Monthly performance review
- Quarterly process improvement
