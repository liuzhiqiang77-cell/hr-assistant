# Materials Support Supervisor Performance Evaluation Framework

## Core Responsibilities
- Managing production planning process
- Managing manufacturing specifications process (including maintenance and development)

## Production Planning Performance
- Check for significant process changes during review period
- Verify site coordination and administrative activity efficiency
- Rating: "good" if sites are well coordinated and activities are efficiently done

## Manufacturing Specifications Performance

### Goal Setting Assessment
Determine if employee defines "clear, concise, and specific goals."

*Negative Indicators:*
- Difficulty setting objectives and key results
- "Mushy" conclusions to system reviews
- Lack of definitive statement on system direction

*Consequence:* Falling into trap of "working on" things without reaching objectives

### Meeting Effectiveness Assessment
Determine if employee confuses holding meetings with actual progress.

*Negative Indicator:* Satisfaction that having a meeting on a subject constitutes progress

*Corrective Action:* Employee must define specific results to accomplish prior to each meeting

## Promotion Eligibility

### Required Capabilities
Must be able to take complex projects (e.g., manufacturing spec system) and show results.

### Required Actions
- Clear and concise breakdown of problems
- Identification of goals
- Establishment of way to achieve those goals

### Independence Requirement
- Employee must be "primary driver"
- Supervisor helps only when needed
- Promotion contingent on demonstrating capability for independent work

## Final Rating
- **Meets Requirements**: Capable of doing current job, acknowledging specific areas need improvement
- **Context Adjustment**: Consider environmental transition difficulties (e.g., finance to manufacturing)
