# Performance Review Variables

## Preparation Variables

- **Worksheet:** The unfiltered list of performance items generated during brainstorming
- **Messages:** Key themes or patterns identified from the worksheet relationships (e.g., 'Good results on planning system')

## Delivery Variables

- **Subordinate_Response:** Verbal and nonverbal feedback from the subordinate during the review
- **Supervisor_Emotion:** The reviewer's feelings such as insecurity, anxiety, or guilt

## Preparation Data Sources

1. Progress reports
2. Performance against quarterly objectives
3. One-on-one meeting notes

## Message Filtering Criteria

- Can the subordinate remember all messages?
- Are messages supported by specific examples?
- Do messages represent different manifestations of the same phenomenon?

## Barrier Indicators During Delivery

- Too emotional to understand clear points
- Preoccupied formulating answers
- Tuned out (defense mechanism)
- Lack of appropriate verbal/nonverbal responses
- Not allowing message reception
