# Materials Support Supervisor Performance Evaluation (Example)

## Context
- **Role**: Materials Support Supervisor
- **Review Period**: 2/82 - 8/82
- **Background Transition**: Finance to Manufacturing

## Core Responsibilities
- Managing the production planning process
- Managing the manufacturing specifications process (including maintenance and development)

## Evaluation Framework

### 1. Production Planning Performance
- **Check for**: Significant process changes during review period
- **Check for**: Site coordination and administrative activity efficiency
- **Rating**: 'good' if sites are well coordinated and activities are efficiently done

### 2. Manufacturing Specifications Performance

#### Goal Setting Assessment
- **Negative Indicators**:
  - Difficulty setting objectives and key results
  - 'Mushy' conclusions to system reviews (e.g., March review)
  - Lack of definitive statement on system direction
- **Consequence**: Falling into trap of 'working on' things without reaching objectives

#### Meeting Effectiveness Assessment
- **Negative Indicator**: Satisfaction that having a meeting on a subject constitutes progress
- **Corrective Action**: Employee must define specific results to accomplish **prior** to each meeting

### 3. Strengths & Background Utilization
- **Learning speed**: Getting up to speed quickly to take over from predecessor
- **Background utilization**: Using prior background (e.g., finance) to assist other areas voluntarily

## Promotion Eligibility
- **Current Status**: Promotion to next management level is denied if criteria not met
- **Required Capabilities**:
  - Ability to take complex projects and show results
  - Clear and concise breakdown of problems
  - Identification of goals
  - Establishment of the way to achieve those goals
- **Independence Requirement**: Employee must be the 'primary driver'; supervisor helps only when needed

## Final Rating Determination
- **Meets Requirements**: Assigned if capable of doing current job, but acknowledging specific areas need improvement
- **Context Adjustment**: Consider environmental transition difficulties (e.g., finance to manufacturing) when interpreting performance

## Signatories Required
- Immediate Supervisor
- Approving Supervisor
- Matrix Manager
- Personnel Administrator
- Employee
