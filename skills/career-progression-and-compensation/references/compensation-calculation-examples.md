# Compensation System Design Examples

## Example 1: Middle Manager Bonus Calculation

**Profile**:
- Total Compensation: $150,000
- Role: Middle Manager
- Bonus Target: 20% of total comp

**Bonus Pool**: $30,000

**3-Factor Split**:
| Factor | Weight | Amount |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Individual Performance | 50% | $15,000 |
| Team Objective | 30% | $9,000 |
| Corporate Performance | 20% | $6,000 |

**Performance Scores**:
- Individual: Exceeds expectations (110%)
- Team: Meets expectations (100%)
- Corporate: Below expectations (80%)

**Final Bonus Calculation**:
- Individual: $15,000 × 1.10 = $16,500
- Team: $9,000 × 1.00 = $9,000
- Corporate: $6,000 × 0.80 = $4,800
- **Total Bonus**: $30,300

## Example 3: Family of Curves Salary Administration

**Starting Point**: All new hires at $80,000

**After 5 Years**:

| Employee | Performance | Salary |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| A | Consistently exceeds | $110,000 |
| B | Mostly exceeds | $100,000 |
| C | Consistently meets | $90,000 |
| D | Sometimes below | $85,000 |
| E | Meets minimum | $80,000 |

**Experience-only curve would place all at**: ~$90,000

**Merit-only curve would place**: A at $120K, E at $70K

**Family of curves**: Balances experience with performance variance
