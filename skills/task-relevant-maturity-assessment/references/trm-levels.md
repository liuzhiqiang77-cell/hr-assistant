# TRM Level Classifications

## Low TRM
- New to task or environment
- Requires detailed instructions
- Frequent check-ins needed
- Close supervision appropriate
- Review at earliest stages

## Medium/Growing TRM
- Gaining experience in specific task
- Can work independently with guidance
- Periodic check-ins appropriate
- Moderate supervision
- Review at key milestones

## High TRM
- Experienced in specific task
- Can operate autonomously
- Minimal supervision needed
- Outcome-focused management
- Spot-check or final review

## TRM Factors Assessment Checklist
- [ ] Relevant education completed?
- [ ] Specific training received?
- [ ] Previous experience with similar task?
- [ ] Demonstrated achievement orientation?
- [ ] Readiness to take responsibility?
- [ ] Environment familiar?
- [ ] Tools/technologies mastered?
