# Detailed TRM Assessment Guide

## Assessment Questions

### Achievement Orientation
- Does the subordinate set ambitious goals for themselves?
- Do they take initiative to improve processes?
- Are they motivated by challenge or comfort?

### Readiness for Responsibility
- Does the subordinate accept accountability for outcomes?
- How do they handle mistakesâ€”blame or learn?
- Do they proactively communicate problems?

### Education and Training
- What formal training is relevant to this task?
- Have they completed necessary certifications or courses?
- Is there a gap between their training and task requirements?

### Experience
- How many times have they performed this specific task?
- What was the outcome of previous attempts?
- Have they handled variations or edge cases?

## TRM Change Indicators

### Factors That Decrease TRM
- New technology or tools
- Changed processes or procedures
- Accelerated pace or deadlines
- Different team or stakeholders
- Expanded scope or complexity

### Factors That Increase TRM
- Successful repetition of the task
- Positive feedback and reinforcement
- Additional training or mentoring
- Handling increasingly complex variations
