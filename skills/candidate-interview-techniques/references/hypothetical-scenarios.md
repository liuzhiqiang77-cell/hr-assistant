# Hypothetical Interview Scenarios

## Manufacturing Example
Walk the candidate through manufacturing steps and ask:
- How would you optimize this process?
- What bottlenecks do you see?
- Calculate the finished cost of a wafer given these parameters...

## Problem-Solving Example
Present a specific business problem:
- A deadline is approaching and key team member is unavailable
- Budget cuts require reducing scope while maintaining quality
- Ask: How would you handle this situation?

Observe:
- How they break down the problem
- What assumptions they make
- Their reasoning process
- The quality of their proposed solution
