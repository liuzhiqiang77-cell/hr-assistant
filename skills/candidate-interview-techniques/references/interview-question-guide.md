# Interview Question Response Guide

## Technical/Skills Questions

**Projects Highly Regarded by Management**
- Look for: Specific examples, impact metrics, recognition received
- Strong answer: Quantifies achievements, explains their value to upper management
- Weak answer: Vague descriptions, takes full credit for team work

**Weaknesses and Improvement**
- Look for: Self-awareness, specific improvement plan, ongoing progress
- Strong answer: Identifies real weakness, shows active steps to address it
- Weak answer: "I work too hard" clich√©, or no concrete improvement plan

## Operational Values Questions

**Why Should We Hire You**
- Look for: Understanding of company needs, relevant skills, confidence without arrogance
- Strong answer: Connects their strengths to company's specific challenges
- Weak answer: Generic statements that could apply to any company

**Readiness for New Job**
- Look for: Evidence of preparation, understanding of role requirements, growth trajectory
- Strong answer: Connects past experiences to new responsibilities
- Weak answer: Focuses only on wanting a promotion, not on capability

## Problem-Solving Questions

**Current Position Problems**
- Look for: Problem identification, solution methodology, self-reflection
- Strong answer: Takes ownership, describes systematic approach, acknowledges prevention opportunities
- Weak answer: Blames others, describes only symptoms not causes

## Direct Questions

When asking blunt questions like "How good are you technically?":
- Confident, specific response = genuine capability
- Hesitant, vague response = likely overestimating ability
- Overconfident without substance = potential competence gap

## Candidate Questions Analysis

**Questions About You/Interviewer:**
- Good sign: Wants to understand reporting structure, management style
- Bad sign: Personal questions inappropriate for interview

**Questions About the Company:**
- Good sign: Research done, asks about strategy, culture, challenges
- Bad sign: Basic questions answered on website, shows lack of preparation

**Questions About the Job:**
- Good sign: Asks about expectations, success metrics, growth opportunities
- Bad sign: Only asks about salary, benefits, hours
