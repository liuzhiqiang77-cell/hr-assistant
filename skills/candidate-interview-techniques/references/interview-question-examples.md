# Extended Interview Question Examples

## Technical/Skills Assessment
- "What specific technologies or methodologies have you mastered?"
- "Describe a complex technical problem you solved and your approach."
- "How do you stay current with developments in your field?"

## Values and Motivation
- "What would make you leave this position after being hired?"
- "Describe your ideal work environment."
- "What type of work do you find most fulfilling?"

## Problem-Solving Scenarios
- Present a broken process and ask how they would diagnose and fix it
- Give a resource-constrained situation and ask for a prioritized solution
- Describe an interpersonal conflict and ask how they would resolve it

## Red Flag Indicators
- Inability to acknowledge specific weaknesses
- Vague or generic achievements
- Blaming others for all problems
- Lack of questions about the role or company
