# Common Resignation Reasons and Solutions

## Reason 1: Lack of Recognition/Appreciation
**Symptoms:**
- "My contributions aren't acknowledged"
- "No one notices when I go above and beyond"
- "I feel invisible"

**Solutions:**
- Public recognition of achievements
- Regular performance feedback
- Inclusion in high-visibility projects
- Title or role adjustment
- Direct acknowledgment from leadership

## Reason 2: Lack of Growth/Development
**Symptoms:**
- "I'm not learning anything new"
- "There's no career path here"
- "I'm stagnating"

**Solutions:**
- Create development plan with clear milestones
- Assign stretch assignments
- Provide training/education budget
- Mentorship opportunities
- Transfer to growth-oriented role

## Reason 3: Poor Management
**Symptoms:**
- "My manager doesn't support me"
- "I'm micromanaged"
- "No clear direction"

**Solutions:**
- Transfer to different team/manager
- Management training for current manager
- Clarify expectations and communication protocols
- Skip-level meetings with senior leadership
- Mediation between employee and manager

## Reason 4: Work-Life Balance Issues
**Symptoms:**
- "I'm burned out"
- "Working too many hours"
- "Can't keep up with demands"

**Solutions:**
- Adjust workload or priorities
- Flexible work arrangements
- Additional resources/support
- Role redesign
- Temporary leave or sabbatical

## Reason 5: Lack of Autonomy
**Symptoms:**
- "I have no say in how I do my work"
- "Everything is dictated to me"
- "My expertise isn't valued"

**Solutions:**
- Increase decision-making authority
- Involve in planning and strategy
- Reduce oversight
- Assign ownership of projects/initiatives
- Transfer to more autonomous role

## Reason 6: Compensation (Secondary)
**Note:** This procedure is NOT for purely money-motivated resignations. However, if compensation is one factor among others:

**Solutions:**
- Market adjustment if truly underpaid
- Performance-based bonus structure
- Equity or profit-sharing
- Non-monetary compensation (perks, benefits)
- **But only if combined with addressing other issues**
