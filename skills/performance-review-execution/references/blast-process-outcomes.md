## 'Blast' Process Outcomes

### Outcome 1: Acceptance
- Subordinate accepts assessment and recommended cure
- Proceed with implementation and monitoring

### Outcome 2: Disagreement with Assessment, Accepts Cure
- Subordinate disagrees with problem diagnosis
- Commits to implementing recommended solution
- Proceed with implementation and monitoring

### Outcome 3: Disagreement and No Commitment
- Subordinate disagrees with assessment
- Refuses to commit to solution
- **Trigger Fallback Procedure**: Use position power to instruct required actions
