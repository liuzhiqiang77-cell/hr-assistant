# Paired Indicator Examples

## Manufacturing
- **Primary**: Inventory Level
- **Counter**: Incidence of Shortages
- **Purpose**: Prevent stockouts while minimizing holding costs

## Project Management
- **Primary**: Completion Date
- **Counter**: Capability/Quality Score
- **Purpose**: Avoid rushing or perfectionism

## Accounts Payable
- **Primary**: # Vouchers Processed
- **Counter**: # Errors Found
- **Purpose**: Balance speed with accuracy

## Custodial Services
- **Primary**: # Square Feet Cleaned
- **Counter**: Quality Rating by Manager
- **Purpose**: Ensure thoroughness, not just coverage

## Sales
- **Primary**: # Orders Obtained
- **Counter**: Customer Satisfaction Score
- **Purpose**: Prevent aggressive selling that damages relationships
