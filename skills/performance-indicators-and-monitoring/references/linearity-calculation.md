# Linearity Indicator Calculation

## Scenario: Recruiting Target
- **Target**: 100 hires by June
- **Current Month**: April (4 months elapsed, 2 remaining)
- **Actual Hires**: 50

## Ideal Straight Line
- Monthly target: 100 / 6 = 16.7 hires/month
- Expected by April: 16.7 × 4 = 67 hires

## Analysis
- **Actual**: 50 hires
- **Expected**: 67 hires
- **Shortfall**: 17 hires
- **Required Rate**: (100 - 50) / 2 = 25 hires/month for remaining period

## Action
Accelerate recruiting efforts immediately—need 50% higher monthly rate to hit target.
