# Monitoring Schedule Examples by TRM Level

## Low TRM (New to Task)
**Frequency:** Daily or multiple times per week
**Monitoring Approach:**
- Review work at earliest possible stage
- Check more details initially
- Provide frequent feedback
- Gradually reduce intensity as performance improves

**Example:** New hire learning to write client proposals
- Day 1-3: Review outline before writing
- Day 4-7: Review rough draft daily
- Week 2: Review every other day
- Week 3+: Review weekly

## Medium TRM (Developing)
**Frequency:** Weekly
**Monitoring Approach:**
- Review at key milestones
- Random sampling of details
- Focus on direction and results

**Example:** Employee familiar with task but new to this type of project
- Review project plan at start
- Check progress weekly
- Review deliverables before submission

## High TRM (Experienced)
**Frequency:** Monthly or as needed
**Monitoring Approach:**
- Review final outputs
- Minimal detail checking
- Focus on strategic alignment

**Example:** Veteran employee handling routine task
- Review monthly summary
- Check in only if issues arise
- Trust their judgment on details
