# One-on-One Content Examples

## Performance Indicators to Review
- Incoming order rates
- Production output metrics
- Project status indicators
- Quality control statistics
- Customer satisfaction scores

## Potential Problem Indicators
- Subordinate seems unusually quiet
- Team morale appears low
- Deadlines being missed without explanation
- Increased conflict between team members
- Declining engagement in meetings

## Hold File Items
- Ideas that need time to develop
- Minor issues that don't require immediate action
- Suggestions for process improvements
- Questions about company policies or procedures
