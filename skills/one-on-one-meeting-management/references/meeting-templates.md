# One-on-One Meeting Templates

## Meeting Outline Template (Subordinate Prep)

### Section 1: Performance Indicators
- Key metrics since last meeting
- Any trouble signals or concerns

### Section 2: Recent Events
- Hiring/People updates
- Organizational changes
- Completed milestones

### Section 3: Current Issues & Problems
- Top 3 challenges
- Blockers or dependencies
- Help needed from supervisor

### Section 4: Future Plans
- Upcoming priorities
- Goals for next period

### Section 5: Hold File Review
- Review items from previous hold file
- Add new non-urgent items

### Section 6: Heart-to-Heart (Optional)
- Career concerns
- Satisfaction/frustration
- Development needs

## Remote Meeting Checklist
- [ ] Outline received and reviewed before meeting
- [ ] Video connection tested
- [ ] Notes document shared
- [ ] Action items documented during meeting
- [ ] Summary notes exchanged after meeting
