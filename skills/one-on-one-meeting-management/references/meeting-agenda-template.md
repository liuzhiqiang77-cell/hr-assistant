# One-on-One Meeting Agenda Template

## Preparation (Subordinate)

Create an outline covering:

### 1. Performance Indicators
- Key metrics since last meeting
- Trends and changes
- Areas of concern

### 2. Recent Events
- Hiring/ staffing updates
- People issues
- Organizational changes
- Completed milestones

### 3. Future Plans
- Upcoming projects
- Resource needs
- Timeline considerations

### 4. Potential Problems
- Issues on your radar
- Intuitive concerns
- Risks you've identified

### 5. Personal/Professional Development
- Satisfaction level
- Frustrations or blockers
- Growth opportunities
- Training needs

### 6. Hold File Items
- Non-urgent issues accumulated since last meeting
- Questions that can wait
- Follow-up items from previous meetings

---

## During the Meeting

1. **Review outline together**
2. **Supervisor asks probing questions** (apply Grove's Principle: "Ask one more question!")
3. **Both parties take notes**
4. **Identify action items and owners**
5. **Schedule next meeting**

---

## After the Meeting

1. **Exchange notes** (especially for remote meetings)
2. **Update Hold File** with non-urgent items
3. **Follow through on commitments**
