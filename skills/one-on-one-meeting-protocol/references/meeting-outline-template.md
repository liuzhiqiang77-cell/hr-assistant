# One-on-One Meeting Outline Template

## Subordinate Preparation

Create an outline before the meeting covering:

### Section 1: Performance Indicators
- Key metrics since last meeting
- Any indicators signaling trouble
- Progress against goals

### Section 2: Recent Events
- Hiring/ staffing updates
- People issues
- Organizational changes
- Completed milestones

### Section 3: Current Issues/Concerns
- Problems blocking progress
- Resource needs
- Decisions needed from supervisor

### Section 4: Future Plans
- Upcoming priorities
- Projects in pipeline
- Anticipated challenges

### Section 5: Hold File Review
- Items carried over from previous meeting
- Non-urgent issues to discuss

### Section 6: Heart-to-Heart (Optional)
- Career development
- Job satisfaction
- Personal work concerns

### Post-Meeting Follow-up

For remote meetings, exchange notes to confirm:
- Action items and owners
- Decisions made
- Next meeting date and time
- Any misunderstandings or clarifications needed
