# Internal Measures - Detailed Examples

## Current Period Contributions

### Collaboration and Teamwork
- Facilitating cross-functional projects
- Supporting colleagues' work
- Sharing knowledge and expertise
- Mentoring junior team members

### Process and Quality
- Improving workflows
- Identifying and resolving issues
- Maintaining quality standards
- Documenting procedures

### Communication and Influence
- Keeping stakeholders informed
- Building consensus
- Representing team effectively
- Managing expectations

## Future-Stage Activities

### People Development
- **Positioning People**: Placing right people in right roles
- **Developing People**: Coaching, training, skill-building
- **Succession Planning**: Preparing backups for critical roles
- **Talent Acquisition**: Contributing to hiring decisions

### Organizational Health
- **Team Culture**: Building positive, productive environment
- **Morale Maintenance**: Preventing disgruntlement and burnout
- **Conflict Resolution**: Addressing issues before they escalate
- **Change Readiness**: Preparing team for future changes

### Capability Building
- **Knowledge Management**: Creating and sharing documentation
- **Tool and System Improvement**: Enhancing work infrastructure
- **Innovation**: Developing new approaches or solutions
- **Strategic Thinking**: Contributing to long-term planning

### Relationship Management
- **Stakeholder Relationships**: Building trust with key partners
- **Customer Relationships**: Strengthening client connections
- **Peer Networks**: Developing external connections
- **Industry Presence**: Representing organization externally

## Assessing Internal Measures

### Observation Methods

1. **Direct Observation**: Watch interactions and behaviors
2. **360-Degree Feedback**: Gather input from multiple sources
3. **Self-Assessment**: Employee's own evaluation
4. **Outcome Tracking**: Monitor results of development activities

### Evaluation Criteria

- **Impact**: How significant is the contribution?
- **Sustainability**: Will benefits continue beyond review period?
- **Scalability**: Can approach be replicated elsewhere?
- **Alignment**: Does it support organizational goals?

### Common Challenges

1. **Subjectivity**: Harder to measure than output
2. **Time Lag**: Benefits may not be immediately visible
3. **Attribution**: Difficult to isolate individual contribution
4. **Invisibility**: Many activities go unnoticed

### Documentation Tips

- Keep specific examples
- Note dates and contexts
- Collect feedback from others
- Link to outcomes when possible
