# Performance Assessment Weighting Examples

## Sales Representative
- **Output measures (80%):** Quota attainment, revenue generated, new clients
- **Internal measures (20%):** Pipeline management, CRM accuracy, team support
- **Rationale:** Results-focused role with clear metrics

## Engineering Manager
- **Output measures (40%):** Projects delivered, technical milestones met
- **Internal measures (60%):** Team development, mentoring, culture building, technical strategy
- **Rationale:** Leadership role with long-term impact focus

## Startup Founder
- **Output measures (70%):** Product launches, funding raised, growth metrics
- **Internal measures (30%):** Team building, company culture, strategic partnerships
- **Rationale:** Survival phase requires strong results but team is critical for future
