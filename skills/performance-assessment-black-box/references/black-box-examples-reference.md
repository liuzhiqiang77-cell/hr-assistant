# Black Box Assessment Examples

## Example 1: Sales Manager

**Output Measures (70%)**
- Sales quota: $1.2M achieved vs. $1M target
- New client acquisition: 8 vs. 5 target
- Revenue growth: 15% vs. 10% target

**Internal Measures (30%)**
- Team training: Conducted weekly coaching sessions
- Pipeline health: Built 6-month qualified pipeline
- Team retention: 0 turnover in critical roles

**Trade-off**: Emphasized current revenue (70/30) due to quarterly pressure

## Example 2: R&D Lead

**Output Measures (40%)**
- Product launches: 2 vs. 3 target
- Patent filings: 4 vs. 3 target
- Bug reduction: 25% vs. 20% target

**Internal Measures (60%)**
- Team development: 3 engineers promoted
- Technical debt reduction: Refactored core architecture
- Knowledge sharing: Established internal tech talks

**Trade-off**: Emphasized future capability (40/60) due to long-term product roadmap

## Example 3: Operations Manager

**Output Measures (50%)**
- Cost reduction: $500K saved vs. $300K target
- Process efficiency: 20% improvement vs. 15% target
- SLA compliance: 98% vs. 95% target

**Internal Measures (50%)**
- Cross-training: 3 team members certified in new systems
- Vendor relationships: Negotiated better long-term contracts
- Team morale: Implemented feedback system, improved satisfaction scores

**Trade-off**: Balanced approach (50/50) for steady-state operations role
