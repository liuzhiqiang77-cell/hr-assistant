# Reference Check Questions

## Opening Questions
- "How long did you work with [candidate]?"
- "What was your working relationship?"
- "Can you describe the candidate's primary responsibilities?"

## Performance Questions
- "What were [candidate]'s biggest strengths?"
- "What areas needed improvement?"
- "Can you describe a project where [candidate] excelled?"
- "Tell me about a time [candidate] faced a challenge and how they handled it."

## Values and Fit Questions
- "How did [candidate] handle conflict or disagreement?"
- "Would you hire [candidate] again? Why or why not?"
- "What type of environment does [candidate] thrive in?"

## Closing Questions
- "Is there anything else I should know?"
- "Would you be comfortable recommending [candidate] for this specific role?"

## Tips for Effective Calls
- Take detailed notes
- Listen for tone and hesitation
- Ask for specific examples
- Pay attention to what is NOT said
- Verify employment dates and titles
