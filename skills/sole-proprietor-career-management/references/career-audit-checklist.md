# Career Audit Checklist

## Competitive Advantage Assessment

### Skills Inventory
- [ ] What are my top 3 core competencies?
- [ ] Are these skills in demand in the current market?
- [ ] How do my skills compare to global competition?
- [ ] What skills am I currently developing?

### Value Proposition
- [ ] What unique value do I provide to my organization?
- [ ] Can I quantify my impact on organizational output?
- [ ] What problems do I solve that others cannot?
- [ ] Would my organization struggle to replace me?

## Connectivity Assessment

### Internal Network
- [ ] Who are my key connections within the organization?
- [ ] Am I included in important decision-making conversations?
- [ ] Do I understand the organization's strategic direction?
- [ ] Am I aware of upcoming changes before they happen?

### External Network
- [ ] Who are my key connections in the industry?
- [ ] Do I participate in professional communities?
- [ ] Am I aware of industry trends and disruptions?
- [ ] Do I have visibility into competitor practices?

## Innovation Assessment

### Learning Activities
- [ ] What new skills have I acquired in the last 6 months?
- [ ] What new technologies have I personally tried?
- [ ] What new processes have I implemented?
- [ ] What failures have I learned from recently?

### Adaptability
- [ ] When was the last time I changed my approach based on new information?
- [ ] Do I embrace or resist change?
- [ ] What would I do if my current role disappeared?
- [ ] What is my backup plan?

## Position Risk Assessment

### Growth Environment
- [ ] Is my organization growing or shrinking?
- [ ] Are there advancement opportunities above me?
- [ ] Are ambitious juniors blocked by my position?
- [ ] What is my boss's perspective on my role?

### Market Position
- [ ] Is my industry growing or declining?
- [ ] Is my company gaining or losing market share?
- [ ] Are there disruptive threats to my business model?
- [ ] How secure is my employment realistically?
