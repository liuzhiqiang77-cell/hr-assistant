# Performance Review Fundamentals - Detailed Reference

## Variable Definitions

### Fundamental Purpose Variables
- **skill_level** (Qualitative Assessment): The current capability and competency of the subordinate
- **motivation_level** (Qualitative Assessment): The drive and engagement of the subordinate

### Time Offset Variables
- **time_offset** (Duration): The lag between the performance of an activity and the visible result
- **present_value** (Financial/Strategic Estimate): Current worth of future-oriented activities

### Potential Trap Variables
- **manager_rating** (Score/Grade): The proposed performance rating for the individual manager
- **organization_rating** (Score/Grade): The performance rating derived from the organization's output and internal measures

### Review Integrity Variables
- **review_delivery_timing** (Enum): Before, During, or After the discussion
- **upward_feedback_status** (String): Advisory (required) vs. Equal (forbidden)

## Result Templates

- **Fundamental Purpose:** A review strategy focused on skill remediation and motivation intensification
- **Time Offset:** An adjusted performance rating that accounts for delayed results and future value
- **Potential Trap:** A corrected rating ensuring Manager Rating <= Organization Rating
- **Review Integrity:** A performance review process that maintains supervisor authority and allows for proper subordinate processing
