# Time Offset Calculation Examples

## Present Value Calculation

When evaluating future-oriented activities:

**Formula:** Estimate future value Ã— discount factor = present value

**Example:**
- Activity: Implementing new design methodology
- Projected benefit: 20% efficiency improvement over 3 years
- Annual savings: $500,000
- Discount rate: 10%
- Present value calculation accounts for time value of money

## Common Time Offsets by Role

**R&D:** 2-5 years between activity and measurable output

**Sales:** 3-6 months between activity and results

**Infrastructure:** 1-3 years between investment and capacity utilization
