# Materials Support Supervisor Performance Evaluation Example

## Context
This example illustrates detailed performance evaluation for a management role transitioning from finance to manufacturing.

## Core Responsibilities
- Managing the production planning process
- Managing the manufacturing specifications process (including maintenance and development)

## Evaluation Criteria

### 1. Production Planning Performance
- Check for significant process changes during review period
- Verify site coordination and administrative activity efficiency
- Rating: "good" if sites are well coordinated and activities are efficiently done

### 2. Manufacturing Specifications Performance

**Goal Setting Assessment**
- **Positive**: Defines clear, concise, and specific goals
- **Negative Indicators**:
  - Difficulty setting objectives and key results
  - "Mushy" conclusions to system reviews
  - Lack of definitive statement on system direction
- **Consequence**: Falling into trap of "working on" things without reaching objectives

**Meeting Effectiveness Assessment**
- **Negative Indicator**: Satisfaction that having a meeting constitutes progress
- **Corrective Action**: Define specific results to accomplish PRIOR to each meeting

### 3. Strengths & Background Utilization
- Evaluate speed of learning
- Evaluate utilization of prior background (e.g., finance) to assist other areas voluntarily

### 4. Promotion Eligibility

**Required Capabilities**
- Must be able to take complex projects and show results
- Clear and concise breakdown of problems
- Identification of goals
- Establishment of the way to achieve those goals

**Independence Requirement**
- Employee must be the "primary driver"
- Supervisor helps only when needed
- Promotion contingent on demonstrating capability for independent work

### 5. Final Rating Determination
- **Meets Requirements**: Capable of doing current job, but acknowledging specific areas need improvement
- **Context Adjustment**: Consider environmental transition difficulties (e.g., finance to manufacturing)

## Signatories Required
- Immediate Supervisor
- Approving Supervisor
- Matrix Manager
- Personnel Administrator
- Employee
