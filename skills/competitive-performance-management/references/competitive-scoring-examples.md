# Competitive Scoring System Examples

## Facility Maintenance Competition

**Setup**
- Assign "building czar" to score each building
- Criteria: Cleanliness, safety, responsiveness to issues
- Weekly scores posted publicly

**Results**
- Immediate improvement without monetary rewards
- Buildings competed for top ranking
- Self-sustaining motivation

## Sales Team Competition

**Setup**
- Individual sales targets
- Weekly leaderboard
- Recognition for top performers

**Rules**
- Clear metrics (revenue, deals closed)
- Fair comparison (similar territories/products)
- Regular feedback on standing

**Coach Behaviors**
- Review performance weekly
- Push for personal best
- No credit taken for team success
- Focus on skill improvement

## Software Development Sprint Competition

**Setup**
- Team velocity tracking
- Story points completed per sprint
- Quality metrics (bugs, rework)

**Rules**
- Consistent story point estimation
- Peer review for quality
- Retrospectives for improvement

**Managing Failure**
- Missed sprint goals analyzed, not punished
- Focus on learning and adjustment
- 50% failure rate normalized as expected

## Warning Signs of Destructive Competition

- Sabotaging teammates
- Hiding information
- Short-term focus at expense of long-term
- Unethical behavior to win
- Toxic team environment

**Intervention Required**
- Realign incentives toward team success
- Emphasize collaboration over individual wins
- Address toxic behavior immediately
