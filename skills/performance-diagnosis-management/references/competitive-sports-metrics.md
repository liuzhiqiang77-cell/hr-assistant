# Competitive Sports Metrics Examples

## Facility Maintenance Competition
- **Metric**: Building inspection score (0-100)
- **Frequency**: Weekly
- **Comparison**: All building managers ranked publicly
- **Reward**: Recognition only (no money)
- **Result**: Immediate improvement in maintenance scores

## Sales Team Competition
- **Metric**: Monthly sales revenue vs. target
- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Comparison**: Individual and team rankings
- **Reward**: Recognition, small perks
- **Result**: Increased sales activity and performance

## Software Development Sprint
- **Metric**: Story points completed, defect rate
- **Frequency**: Per sprint (2 weeks)
- **Comparison**: Team velocity vs. historical average
- **Reward**: Team recognition, celebration
- **Result**: Improved delivery predictability

## Customer Service Response
- **Metric**: Average response time, customer satisfaction score
- **Frequency**: Daily tracking, weekly review
- **Comparison**: Individual agent performance
- **Reward**: Top performer recognition
- **Result**: Faster response times, higher satisfaction

## Key Principles for Effective Metrics
1. **Objective**: Clearly measurable, not subjective
2. **Visible**: Everyone can see their standing
3. **Frequent**: Regular feedback loops
4. **Fair**: Within individual's control
5. **Simple**: Easy to understand and track
