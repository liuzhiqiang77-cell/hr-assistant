# Interview Question Bank

## Recommended Core Questions

### Performance and Recognition
- "Describe some projects that were highly regarded by your management, especially by the levels above your immediate supervisor."

### Self-Assessment
- "What are your weaknesses? How are you working to eliminate them?"

### Value Proposition
- "Convince me why my company should hire you."

### Problem-Solving
- "What are some of the problems you are encountering in your current position? How are you going about solving them? What could you have done to prevent them from cropping up?"

### Readiness
- "Why do you think you're ready for this new job?"

### Achievements
- "What do you consider your most significant achievements? Why were they important to you?"

## Direct Assessment Questions

- "How good are you technically?"
- "What's the most complex technical challenge you've faced?"
- "Rate your proficiency in [specific skill] from 1-10. Why that number?"

## Hypothetical Scenario Examples

### For Technical Roles
- Walk through the process of [manufacturing step/development cycle]
- Calculate the finished cost of a product given these raw material costs
- How would you debug this system error?

### For Management Roles
- An employee is underperforming. Walk me through your approach.
- You have conflicting priorities from two stakeholders. How do you handle it?

## Assessment Indicators

### Positive Indicators
- Confident, direct answers to blunt questions
- Specific examples with measurable outcomes
- Questions that show research about the company
- Recognition of past mistakes and clear improvement plans

### Caution Indicators
- Vague or generic responses
- Defensive reactions to direct questions
- Inability to walk through processes step-by-step
- No substantive questions about the role
