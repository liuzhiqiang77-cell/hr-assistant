# Hypothetical Scenario Examples

## Manufacturing Context
- Walk through the complete manufacturing process for a product
- Ask candidate to calculate finished cost of a wafer or component
- Present a production bottleneck and ask for solution approach

## Problem-Solving Scenarios
- Present a realistic work problem relevant to the role
- Ask candidate to outline their step-by-step approach
- Observe how they identify root causes and propose solutions

## Process Walkthroughs
- Describe a typical workflow in your organization
- Ask candidate where they would identify risks or improvements
- Assess their ability to see the big picture and operational details

## Key Assessment Points
- Does the candidate ask clarifying questions?
- Can they derive principles from unfamiliar situations?
- Do they approach problems systematically?
- Can they explain their reasoning clearly?
