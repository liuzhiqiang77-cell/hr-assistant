# Reference Checking Guide

## Preparation
- Have candidate's resume and interview notes handy
- Prepare specific questions based on interview concerns
- Schedule adequate time (30+ minutes recommended)

## Opening the Call
- Introduce yourself and your role
- Explain the purpose of the call
- Establish context about the position

## Building Rapport Techniques

### If You Know the Reference
- Acknowledge your relationship
- Use informal tone appropriately
- Leverage existing trust

### If the Reference is a Stranger
- Find common ground (industry, geography, mutual contacts)
- Share relevant context about your organization
- Be patient in building the relationship
- Start with easier questions before probing deeper

## Information to Verify
- Job titles and dates of employment
- Responsibilities and scope of role
- Specific projects and achievements
- Strengths and areas for development
- Reason for leaving
- Rehire recommendation

## Red Flags to Watch For
- Reluctance to provide specific examples
- Vague or non-committal responses
- Inconsistencies with candidate's statements
- Hesitation on rehire question
- Excessive praise without specifics

## Closing the Call
- Thank the reference for their time
- Offer to reciprocate in the future
- Leave door open for follow-up questions
