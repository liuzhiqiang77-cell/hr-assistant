# Compensation Calculation Examples

## Bonus Calculation Example

**Middle Manager:**
- Total Compensation: $150,000
- Bonus Target: 20% of total comp
- Bonus Amount: $30,000

**Bonus Split (3-Factor):**
- Individual Performance (40%): $12,000
- Team Objective (30%): $9,000
- Corporate Financial (30%): $9,000

## Family of Curves Example

**Base Salary Curve:**
- Year 1: $80,000 (all start here)
- Year 2: $88,000 (10% increase for meets)
- Year 3: $96,800 (10% increase for meets)

**Performance Adjustments:**
- Exceeds: +15% above curve
- Meets: On curve
- Below: +5% or 0% above curve

**Year 3 Examples:**
- High Performer: $111,320 ($96,800 × 1.15)
- Average Performer: $96,800
- Below Average: $101,640 ($96,800 × 1.05)
