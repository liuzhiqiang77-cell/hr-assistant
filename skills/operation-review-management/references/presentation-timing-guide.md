# Operation Review Presentation Timing

## Rule of Thumb

**4 minutes per visual aid** (tables, numbers, graphics)

## Time Calculation Examples

| Visual Aids | Total Time |
|-------------|------------|
| 5 | 20 minutes |
| 10 | 40 minutes |
| 15 | 60 minutes |
| 20 | 80 minutes |

## Time Allocation Breakdown

For each visual aid:
- 2-3 minutes: Presentation and explanation
- 1-2 minutes: Questions and discussion

## Presenter Monitoring Techniques

- Watch facial expressions for confusion
- Observe body language for engagement
- Adjust speed if audience appears lost
- Clarify points if questions arise frequently

## Audience Engagement Best Practices

- Maintain eye contact with presenter
- Ask clarifying questions
- Offer constructive comments
- Correct factual errors respectfully and publicly
