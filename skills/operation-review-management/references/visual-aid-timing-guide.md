# Visual Aid Timing Guide

## Rule of Thumb
**4 minutes per visual aid**

This includes:
- Presentation time
- Discussion time
- Questions and answers

## Calculation Examples

| Visual Aids | Total Time |
|-------------|------------|
| 5 overheads | 20 minutes |
| 10 overheads | 40 minutes |
| 15 overheads | 60 minutes |

## Visual Aid Types
- Tables
- Numbers
- Graphics
- Charts

## Presenter Tips
- Engage eyes and ears simultaneously
- Use color pen or pointer for emphasis
- Monitor audience like a hawk
- Adjust speed based on facial expressions and body language
