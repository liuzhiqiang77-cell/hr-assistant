# Operation Review Role Details

## Organizing Manager Checklist

### Before the Review
- [ ] Select topics and decide emphasis level
- [ ] Determine required level of detail for each topic
- [ ] Schedule presenters with time allocations
- [ ] Book appropriate room with capacity for all attendees
- [ ] Verify and test visual equipment
- [ ] Send invitations with clear purpose and expectations
- [ ] Provide presenters with guidelines on visual aids and timing

### During the Review
- [ ] Start on time
- [ ] Introduce each presenter and topic
- [ ] Monitor time discreetly
- [ ] Use gestures (not verbal interruptions) to pace presenters
- [ ] Manage transitions between presentations
- [ ] Ensure all scheduled topics are covered

## Reviewing Manager Checklist

### Before the Review
- [ ] Commit to attending and participating fully
- [ ] DO NOT preview any presentation materials
- [ ] Prepare questions that probe thinking and assumptions

### During the Review
- [ ] Arrive on time and stay engaged
- [ ] Model active participation behaviors
- [ ] Ask probing questions (not just factual ones)
- [ ] Share relevant insights and experiences
- [ ] Encourage quiet audience members to speak
- [ ] Provide constructive feedback on thinking process
- [ ] Impart organizational spirit and values through comments

### After the Review
- [ ] Follow up on action items
- [ ] Coach presenters one-on-one if needed

## Presenter Checklist

### Before the Review
- [ ] Understand your time allocation
- [ ] Calculate visual aids: (Time allocated รท 4) = max aids
- [ ] Prepare clear, readable visual aids
- [ ] Practice timing with your materials
- [ ] Anticipate potential questions

### During the Review
- [ ] Make eye contact with audience
- [ ] Use pointer or color pen to highlight key points
- [ ] Watch for confusion (furrowed brows, leaning forward)
- [ ] Watch for disengagement (checking watches, looking away)
- [ ] Adjust pace based on audience signals
- [ ] Clarify points when you see confusion
- [ ] Welcome questions and feedback
- [ ] Stay within your time allocation

## Audience Member Checklist

### Before the Review
- [ ] Review the agenda and prepare relevant questions
- [ ] Commit to being fully present

### During the Review
- [ ] Arrive on time
- [ ] Sit where you can see and hear clearly
- [ ] Maintain eye contact with presenter
- [ ] Take notes on key points and questions
- [ ] Ask at least one question or make one comment
- [ ] Correct factual errors when you notice them
- [ ] Demonstrate engagement through body language
- [ ] Avoid distractions (phones, papers, side conversations)
