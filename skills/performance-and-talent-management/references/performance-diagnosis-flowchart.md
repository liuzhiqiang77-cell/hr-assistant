# Performance Diagnosis Flowchart

```
Employee Not Performing
        |
        v
Is the role clearly defined?
        |
        +-- No --> Define role and expectations --> Reassess
        |
        +-- Yes --> Can they do the job?
                     |
                     +-- No (Capability Issue) -->
                     |    |
                     |    +-- Provide training
                     |    +-- Offer coaching/mentoring
                     |    +-- Adjust job requirements
                     |    +-- Consider reassignment
                     |    |
                     |    v
                     |   Reassess Performance
                     |
                     +-- Yes --> Will they do the job?
                          |
                          +-- No (Motivation Issue) -->
                          |    |
                          |    +-- Provide motivation
                          |    +-- Address incentives
                          |    +-- Clarify consequences
                          |    +-- Performance improvement plan
                          |    |
                          |    v
                          |   Reassess Performance
                          |
                          +-- Yes --> Is there a barrier?
                                       |
                                       +-- Yes --> Remove barrier
                                       |
                                       +-- No --> Consider termination
```

## Diagnostic Questions

### Capability Assessment
- Does the employee have necessary skills?
- Have they received adequate training?
- Do they understand expectations?
- Are resources/tools available?
- Is the workload reasonable?

### Motivation Assessment
- Does the employee understand impact of poor performance?
- Are consequences clear?
- Is there incentive to perform well?
- Are there personal issues affecting work?
- Is the employee engaged in their work?
