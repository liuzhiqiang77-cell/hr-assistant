# Performance Indicator Examples

## Functional Area Examples

### Sales Management
**Primary Metric**: Orders obtained per month
**Counter Metric**: 
- Return rate (%)
- Customer satisfaction score
- Profit margin per order

**Rationale**: Prevents salespeople from pushing unprofitable orders or misrepresenting products.

### Production Management
**Primary Metric**: Units produced per shift
**Counter Metric**:
- Defect rate (%)
- Rework hours
- Equipment downtime

**Rationale**: Prevents sacrificing quality for volume.

### Customer Service
**Primary Metric**: Tickets resolved per day
**Counter Metric**:
- Customer satisfaction rating
- First-contact resolution rate
- Escalation rate

**Rationale**: Prevents rushing through tickets without solving problems.

### Software Development
**Primary Metric**: Features delivered per sprint
**Counter Metric**:
- Bug count per release
- Code review coverage
- Technical debt ratio

**Rationale**: Prevents delivering buggy code to meet velocity targets.

### Human Resources
**Primary Metric**: Positions filled per month
**Counter Metric**:
- 90-day retention rate
- Quality of hire rating
- Time to productivity

**Rationale**: Prevents hiring unqualified candidates just to fill positions.

### Finance/Accounts Payable
**Primary Metric**: Vouchers processed per day
**Counter Metric**:
- Error rate (%)
- Late payment penalties
- Discount capture rate

**Rationale**: Prevents processing errors or missing early payment discounts.

### Facilities/Custodial
**Primary Metric**: Square feet cleaned per shift
**Counter Metric**:
- Quality inspection score
- Complaints received
- Re-clean requests

**Rationale**: Prevents rushing through cleaning without thoroughness.

### IT Support
**Primary Metric**: Tickets closed per day
**Counter Metric**:
- First-time fix rate
- Customer satisfaction
- Recurrence rate

**Rationale**: Prevents temporary fixes that lead to repeat issues.

## Strategic Level Examples

### Company Performance
**Primary Metric**: Revenue growth
**Counter Metric**:
- Profit margin
- Cash flow
- Customer retention

**Rationale**: Prevents growing revenue unprofitably.

### Innovation
**Primary Metric**: New products launched
**Counter Metric**:
- R&D ROI
- Success rate (% profitable)
- Time to market

**Rationale**: Prevents launching products that don't succeed.

### Market Expansion
**Primary Metric**: New markets entered
**Counter Metric**:
- Market share achieved
- Profitability by market
- Brand awareness

**Rationale**: Prevents entering markets without achieving meaningful presence.
