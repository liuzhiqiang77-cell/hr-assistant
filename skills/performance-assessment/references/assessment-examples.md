# Performance Assessment Examples

## Example 1: Sales Manager (Output-Heavy)

**Context:** End of quarter, revenue critical

**Output Measures (90% weighting):**
- Achieved 110% of sales quota
- Closed 3 major enterprise deals
- Increased territory revenue by 25%

**Internal Measures (10% weighting):**
- Mentored 2 junior reps
- Maintained customer relationships

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- Sacrificed some mentoring time to focus on closing deals
- Acceptable given quarterly revenue pressure

**Final Assessment:** Exceeds expectations

---

## Example 2: Engineering Manager (Balanced)

**Context:** Stable period, team growth phase

**Output Measures (50% weighting):**
- Delivered 4 major features on time
- Maintained 99.9% uptime
- Reduced bug count by 15%

**Internal Measures (50% weighting):**
- Hired and onboarded 3 engineers
- Improved team satisfaction scores
- Established code review processes
- Created technical documentation for future teams

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- Balanced delivery with team building
- Investment in processes will pay dividends next period

**Final Assessment:** Meets expectations

---

## Example 3: HR Director (Internal-Heavy)

**Context:** Post-merger integration, cultural alignment

**Output Measures (20% weighting):**
- Completed all hiring requisitions
- Maintained compliance standards

**Internal Measures (80% weighting):**
- Designed and implemented cultural integration program
- Reduced turnover from 25% to 12%
- Established cross-functional teams
- Created succession plans for key roles
- Improved employee engagement scores by 30 points

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- Focus on long-term organizational health
- Output measures less relevant during transformation

**Final Assessment:** Exceeds expectations

---

## Common Internal Measure Categories

**People Development:**
- Mentoring and coaching
- Training delivery
- Career progression support
- Succession planning

**Organizational Health:**
- Process improvement
- Culture building
- Team cohesion
- Conflict resolution

**Future Capability:**
- Knowledge management
- Documentation
- Tool and system development
- Strategic planning

**Risk Mitigation:**
- Compliance maintenance
- Issue prevention
- Stakeholder management
- Crisis preparation
