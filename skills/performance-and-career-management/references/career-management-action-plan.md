# Sole Proprietor Career Management: Action Plan

## Weekly Career Maintenance

### Monday: Value Assessment
- [ ] Review last week's work: What real value did I add?
- [ ] Identify this week's highest-impact activities
- [ ] Ask: "What can I do this week that only I can do?"

### Tuesday: Connectivity Check
- [ ] Read industry news and trends
- [ ] Connect with someone outside my immediate team
- [ ] Update my knowledge of company strategy and direction

### Wednesday: Skill Development
- [ ] Spend 1 hour learning something new
- [ ] Try a new tool or technique in actual work
- [ ] Share learning with team

### Thursday: Innovation Experiment
- [ ] Test one new approach to a routine task
- [ ] Propose one improvement to a process
- [ ] Seek feedback on the experiment

### Friday: Competitive Advantage Review
- [ ] What did I learn this week that makes me more valuable?
- [ ] What skills am I developing that others aren't?
- [ ] What's my plan for next week?

## Monthly Career Audit

### Market Position
- [ ] What are my top 3 skills?
- [ ] How do these compare to market demand?
- [ ] What's emerging in my field that I should learn?

### Internal Position
- [ ] Am I blocking anyone's advancement?
- [ ] What's my next move within the company?
- [ ] Am I visible to decision-makers?

### External Options
- [ ] Update resume/LinkedIn
- [ ] Research market rates for my role
- [ ] Identify 3 companies where I'd want to work

## Quarterly Strategic Review

### Competitive Analysis
- [ ] Who are my global competitors?
- [ ] What do they offer that I don't?
- [ ] How can I differentiate myself?

### Value Proposition
- [ ] What problem do I solve better than anyone else?
- [ ] Who would pay for this solution?
- [ ] How can I demonstrate this value?

### Career Path Options
- [ ] Stay and grow in current role
- [ ] Move to different role in current company
- [ ] Move to new company
- [ ] Change industries
- [ ] Start own business

## Annual Career Reset

### Skills Inventory
- List all skills and rate proficiency (1-10)
- Identify gaps between current and desired skills
- Create learning plan for next year

### Network Assessment
- Who are my most valuable connections?
- Who should I connect with this year?
- How can I provide value to my network?

### Financial Position
- What's my current income?
- What's my target income?
- What's the gap and how do I close it?

### Risk Assessment
- What could make me obsolete?
- What's my backup plan?
- How quickly could I find new work?
