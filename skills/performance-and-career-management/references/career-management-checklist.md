# Career Management Action Checklist

## Daily Practices
- [ ] Identify one way to add real value today
- [ ] Connect with at least one peer outside immediate team
- [ ] Learn or try something new (technology, technique, method)
- [ ] Assess: Did my activities increase my organization's output?

## Weekly Practices
- [ ] Review industry news and trends
- [ ] Update skills inventory and identify gaps
- [ ] Seek feedback from multiple sources
- [ ] Network with someone outside current organization

## Monthly Practices
- [ ] Evaluate competitive position in job market
- [ ] Update career development plan
- [ ] Assess internal threats (junior employees, organizational changes)
- [ ] Identify new skills to acquire

## Quarterly Practices
- [ ] Comprehensive self-assessment (3 questions)
- [ ] Review and adjust career strategy
- [ ] Update resume and professional profile
- [ ] Explore external opportunities (even if not actively looking)

## Annual Practices
- [ ] Complete career SWOT analysis
- [ ] Set specific skill development goals
- [ ] Evaluate long-term career trajectory
- [ ] Consider industry trends and future-proofing

## The 3 Questions Deep Dive

### Value Addition
- **Red flags**: Passing information without analysis, attending meetings without contribution, administrative tasks without improvement
- **Green flags**: Process improvements, cost savings, revenue generation, team capability building

### Connectivity
- **Red flags**: Working in isolation, waiting for information to be shared, unaware of company direction
- **Green flags**: Active information sharing, cross-functional relationships, industry awareness

### Innovation
- **Red flags**: Resistance to change, relying on old methods, waiting for others to innovate
- **Green flags**: Experimenting with new tools, proposing improvements, early adoption of technology
