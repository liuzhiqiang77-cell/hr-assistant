# Career Self-Assessment Template

## Weekly Review Questions

### Value Addition
- What specific value did I add this week?
- Did I merely pass information along, or did I improve processes?
- How did my actions increase the output of my team/organization?

### Connectivity
- What key developments did I learn about this week (inside and outside the company)?
- Who did I connect with to stay informed?
- Am I waiting for others to interpret events, or am I directly plugged in?

### Innovation
- What new idea, technique, or technology did I personally try this week?
- What did I read about that I should actually try?
- Am I at risk of being re-engineered out of my workplace?

## Monthly Competitive Advantage Review

- What skills have I enhanced this month?
- What new capabilities have I developed?
- How has my value to the organization increased?
- What competitive threats do I face (internal and external)?
- What will I learn next month?
