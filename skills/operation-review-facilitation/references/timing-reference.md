# Operation Review Timing Reference

## Rule of Thumb Calculation

**Formula**: 4 minutes per visual aid

### Examples
- 5 visual aids = 20 minutes total
- 10 visual aids = 40 minutes total
- 15 visual aids = 60 minutes total

### Visual Aid Types
- Tables
- Numbers/charts
- Graphics
- Overhead transparencies

### Timekeeper Responsibilities
- Use inconspicuous gestures to signal time
- Keep presentations moving to prevent running out of time
- Balance presentation time with discussion time
