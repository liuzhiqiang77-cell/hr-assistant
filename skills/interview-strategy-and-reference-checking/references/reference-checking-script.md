# Reference Checking Script

## Opening the Call

"Hello [Name], this is [Your Name] from [Company]. I'm calling about [Candidate Name] who listed you as a reference. Do you have 20-30 minutes to chat?"

## Building Rapport (First 10-15 minutes)

1. **Establish common ground**
   - "How long have you been at [Company]?"
   - "Did you work with [Candidate] on the [Project] initiative?"
   - "I understand you both came from [Industry/Company] originally?"

2. **Set context**
   - "We're considering [Candidate] for [Role]. I'd value your honest assessment."
   - "Everything we discuss will be kept confidential."

## Standard Questions

1. **Working Relationship**
   - "How long did you work with [Candidate]?"
   - "What was your reporting relationship?"

2. **Strengths**
   - "What were [Candidate]'s greatest strengths?"
   - "What would you say they're exceptionally good at?"

3. **Areas for Improvement**
   - "What areas did [Candidate] need to develop?"
   - "What would you advise us to help them with in the first 90 days?"

4. **Specific Examples**
   - "Can you give me an example of a challenging situation [Candidate] handled well?"
   - "Tell me about a time they struggled. How did they respond?"

5. **Performance Relative to Peers**
   - "How would you compare [Candidate] to others in similar roles?"
   - "Top 10%? Top quarter? Average?"

## Deep-Dive Questions (After Rapport Established, Last 10-15 Minutes)

1. **Re-hiring Decision**
   - "If you had the opportunity, would you hire [Candidate] again?"
   - If hesitation: "What gives you pause?"

2. **Cultural Fit**
   - "How did [Candidate] fit with your team culture?"
   - "Who did they work best with? Who did they struggle with?"

3. **Red Flags**
   - "Is there anything about [Candidate] that I haven't asked about but should know?"
   - "What surprised you most when working with them?"

4. **Reference's Perspective**
   - "What do you think [Candidate] is looking for in their next role?"
   - "Where do you see them thriving? Where would they struggle?"

## Closing

"Thank you for your time. Is there anything else you'd like to add?"
