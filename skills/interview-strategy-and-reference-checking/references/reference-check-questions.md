# Reference Check Question Bank

## Rapport-Building Questions
- How long have you known [candidate]?
- In what capacity did you work together?
- Can you tell me about your experience working with them?

## Performance Verification Questions
- What were [candidate]'s primary responsibilities?
- Can you describe a project where [candidate] excelled?
- What areas did [candidate] struggle with?
- How did [candidate] handle pressure or deadlines?
- Would you hire [candidate] again? Why or why not?

## Probing Questions
- Can you give me a specific example of [skill/behavior]?
- How did [candidate] handle conflict with team members?
- What was [candidate]'s biggest contribution to your team?
- In what ways could [candidate] have improved?

## Closing Questions
- Is there anything else I should know about [candidate]?
- Would you recommend [candidate] for this type of role?
