# Sole Proprietor Mindset: Context

## Competitive Landscape Ratios

Fellow workers are not primary rivals; they are outnumbered by people working for competing organizations:
- Ratios of 1000:1
- Ratios of 100,000:1
- Ratios of 1,000,000:1

## Era Context

Post-1980s globalization and Information Revolution context (Andrew S. Grove, Intel).

## Goal

Manage your career to avoid becoming a casualty of globalization and the information revolution.
