# Linearity Indicator Scenarios

## Scenario A: Recruiting
- **Target**: 100 hires by June
- **April Progress**: 30 hires (below ideal line of ~50)
- **Analysis**: Need 70 hires in May-June (35/month vs. 25/month average)
- **Action**: Accelerate recruiting efforts immediately

## Scenario B: Manufacturing
- **Target**: 1,000 units by month end
- **Pattern**: 200 units in first 3 weeks, 400 units in final week
- **Analysis**: Inefficient resource use; back-loaded production
- **Risk**: Breakdown in final week could miss goal
- **Action**: Rebalance production schedule across month
