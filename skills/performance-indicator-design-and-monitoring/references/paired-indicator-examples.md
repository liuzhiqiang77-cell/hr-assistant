# Paired Indicator Examples

## Manufacturing
- **Primary**: Inventory Level
- **Counter**: Incidence of Shortages
- **Purpose**: Prevent driving inventory too low (creates shortages) or too high (waste)

## Project Management
- **Primary**: Completion Date
- **Counter**: Capability/Quality
- **Purpose**: Prevent rushing (poor quality) or perfectionism (missed deadlines)

## Administrative Functions

### Accounts Payable
- **Primary**: # Vouchers processed
- **Counter**: # Errors found

### Custodial Services
- **Primary**: # Square feet cleaned
- **Counter**: Quality rating by senior manager

### Sales
- **Primary**: # Orders obtained
- **Counter**: # Returns or customer complaints
