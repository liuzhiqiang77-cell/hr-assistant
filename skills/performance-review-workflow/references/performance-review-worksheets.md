## Delivery Checklist

### Before the Review
- [ ] Messages finalized
- [ ] Supporting examples prepared
- [ ] Specific, actionable feedback ready
- [ ] Emotionally prepared for difficult conversations

### During the Review: The Three L's

#### LEVEL - Be Totally Frank
- [ ] Am I being completely honest?
- [ ] Am I avoiding saying anything uncomfortable?
- [ ] Am I balancing praise and criticism appropriately?
- [ ] Am I being straightforward with positive feedback too?

#### LISTEN - Ensure Understanding
- [ ] Am I watching their reactions?
- [ ] Are they showing appropriate nonverbal responses?
- [ ] Are they too emotional to understand?
- [ ] Are they formulating answers instead of listening?
- [ ] Have they tuned out?
- [ ] Have I verified they understood the message?
- [ ] Did I rephrase if they didn't understand?

#### LEAVE YOURSELF OUT - Focus on Subordinate
- [ ] Am I talking about my feelings?
- [ ] Am I bringing in my own insecurities?
- [ ] Am I feeling guilty about delivering bad news?
- [ ] Am I making this about me instead of them?
- [ ] Is my focus entirely on their performance?

### After the Review
- [ ] Clear action items documented
- [ ] Follow-up scheduled
- [ ] Notes written while fresh
