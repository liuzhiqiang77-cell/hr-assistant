# Promotion and Recycling - Detailed Reference

## Variable Definitions

### Promotion Variables
- **performance_rating** (string): Current assessment of employee output ('meets requirements' or 'exceeds requirements')
- **job_level** (integer): Current tier or difficulty of the assigned role

### Recycling Variables
- **performance_duration** (duration): Time spent performing below average in the new role
- **previous_role_success** (boolean): Confirmation that the employee performed well in the prior role

## Result Templates

- **Promotion:** Promote employee to next job level OR maintain current level if performance is 'meets requirements'
- **Recycling:** Employee is demoted to previous successful role with management support, retaining them for future success
