# Career Development Framework

## Competitive Advantage Assessment

### Skills Inventory
- Technical skills (current and emerging)
- Industry knowledge
- Leadership capabilities
- Network strength
- Learning agility

### Market Analysis
- Demand for your skills
- Supply of competing talent
- Geographic mobility requirements
- Industry growth trajectory
- Compensation benchmarks

## Career Strategy Development

### Short-term (0-12 months)
- Identify skill gaps
- Create learning plan
- Expand internal network
- Document achievements
- Seek high-visibility projects

### Medium-term (1-3 years)
- Pursue certifications or advanced education
- Build external reputation (speaking, writing)
- Develop mentor relationships
- Explore lateral moves for breadth
- Consider promotion opportunities

### Long-term (3-5+ years)
- Define career end-state
- Identify required experience
- Build executive presence
- Cultivate strategic relationships
- Plan industry or role transitions

## Leadership Development Path

### From Individual Contributor to Manager
- Learn delegation
- Develop coaching skills
- Understand business metrics
- Build team management capabilities

### From Manager to Executive
- Develop strategic thinking
- Build cross-functional influence
- Master financial acumen
- Cultivate external stakeholder relationships

### Personal Branding
- Define unique value proposition
- Establish thought leadership
- Build consistent online presence
- Seek speaking and writing opportunities
- Develop executive communication skills
