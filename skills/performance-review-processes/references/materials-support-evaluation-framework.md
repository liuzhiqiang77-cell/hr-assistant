# Materials Support Supervisor Evaluation Framework

## Context
- Role: Managing production planning process and manufacturing specifications process
- Transition: Finance to Manufacturing background
- Review Period: Example 2/82 - 8/82

## Core Responsibilities Assessment

### 1. Production Planning Performance
**Check for:**
- Significant process changes during review period
- Site coordination effectiveness
- Administrative activity efficiency

**Rating Criteria:**
- 'Good' if sites are well coordinated and activities efficiently done

### 2. Manufacturing Specifications Performance

**Goal Setting Assessment:**
- **Positive**: Defines clear, concise, specific goals
- **Negative Indicators**:
  - Difficulty setting objectives and key results
  - 'Mushy' conclusions to system reviews (e.g., March review)
  - Lack of definitive statement on system direction
- **Consequence**: Falling into trap of 'working on' things without reaching objectives

**Meeting Effectiveness Assessment:**
- **Negative Indicator**: Satisfaction that having a meeting constitutes progress
- **Corrective Action**: Must define specific results to accomplish PRIOR to each meeting

### 3. Strengths & Background Utilization
**Evaluate:**
- Speed of learning (getting up to speed quickly)
- Utilization of prior background (e.g., finance) to assist other areas voluntarily
- Example: Helping Purchasing group sort out finance problems

## Promotion Eligibility Determination

**Current Status:**
- Promotion to next management level denied if specific criteria not met

**Required Capabilities:**
- Ability to take complex projects (e.g., manufacturing spec system) and show results
- Clear and concise breakdown of problems
- Identification of goals
- Establishment of the way to achieve those goals

**Independence Requirement:**
- Employee must be 'primary driver'
- Supervisor helps only when needed
- Promotion contingent on demonstrating capability for independent work

## Final Rating Determination

**Meets Requirements:**
- Assigned if capable of doing current job
- Acknowledging specific areas need improvement (goal setting, defining ways of accomplishing tasks)

**Context Adjustment:**
- Consider environmental transition difficulties (e.g., finance to manufacturing)
- Adjust interpretation of performance based on transition context

## Required Signatories
- Immediate Supervisor
- Approving Supervisor
- Matrix Manager
- Personnel Administrator
- Employee
