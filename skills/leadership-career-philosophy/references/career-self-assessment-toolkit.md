# Career Self-Assessment Toolkit

## The 3-Question Daily Check-In

### Question 1: Value Addition

**Audit Questions:**
- What did I produce today that advanced my organization's output?
- Did I merely pass information along, or did I add value?
- What would happen if my work disappeared for a week?

**Action Indicators:**
- ✓ Created something new
- ✓ Solved a problem that existed
- ✓ Improved a process
- ✓ Helped someone else be more productive

**Warning Signs:**
- ✗ Forwarded emails without adding insight
-- ✗ Attended meetings without contributing
- ✗ Performed routine tasks without improvement

### Question 2: Connectivity

**Network Assessment:**
- Who are the 5 most "plugged-in" people in my organization?
- Am I in regular communication with them?
- What industry trends am I tracking this week?
- What competitive intelligence have I gathered recently?

**Connection Actions:**
- Schedule 1:1s with key nodes in your network
- Join or start cross-functional initiatives
- Subscribe to industry publications and actually read them
- Attend relevant conferences or virtual events

**Warning Signs:**
- ✗ Learning about company news through official announcements only
- ✗ No regular contact with peers outside immediate team
- ✗ Unsure of competitors' recent moves

### Question 3: Innovation

**Personal Innovation Tracker:**
- New tools tried this month: _______
- New processes tested: _______
- Skills being learned: _______
- Experiments conducted: _______

**Innovation Actions:**
- Volunteer for pilot projects
- Propose and test new approaches
- Take online courses and apply learning
- Read about new technologies and implement small proofs of concept

**Warning Signs:**
- ✗ Using same tools as 2 years ago
- ✗ Following same processes "because that's how we do it"
- ✗ No new skills learned in past 6 months

## Internal Threat Assessment

### Slow/No-Growth Environment Checklist

**Assess Your Situation:**
- Is your team/department growing in budget or headcount?
- Are there ambitious junior employees reporting to you?
- Have you been in your current role for 3+ years without promotion?
- Are you blocking others' advancement?

**Strategic Options:**
- **Move Up**: Seek promotion to higher role
- **Move On**: Transition to different company or industry
- **Move Over**: Transfer to different part of organization
- **Create Growth**: Expand role or create new opportunities

### Preparation for Transition

**Always Keep Ready:**
- Updated resume/portfolio
- Active network of contacts
- Marketable skills demonstrated through recent work
- Financial runway for potential career change
- Clear value proposition and differentiators
