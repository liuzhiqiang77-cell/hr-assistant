## Analysis Example

### June Forecast Evolution
- January forecast for June: 150
- February forecast for June: 155
- March forecast for June: 160
- April forecast for June: 165
- May forecast for June: 170
- June forecast for June: 175

**Interpretation**: Consistently upward trend indicates improving business outlook.

### Variance Detection

If forecasts show:
- **Consistent upward revision**: Business better than expected
- **Consistent downward revision**: Business deteriorating
- **Erratic changes**: Uncertainty in market conditions
- **Stable forecasts**: High confidence in predictions
