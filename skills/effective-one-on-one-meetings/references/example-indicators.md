# Example Performance Indicators for One-on-Ones

## Operational Indicators

- Production output rates
- Quality metrics (defect rates, rework)
- Schedule adherence
- Inventory levels
- Equipment uptime

## Business Indicators

- Incoming order rates
- Sales figures
- Customer satisfaction scores
- Budget variance
- Project milestones

## People Indicators

- Team attendance
- Turnover rates
- Training completion
- Employee engagement scores

## Trouble Signals (Priority Attention)

- Declining trends in any metric
- Missed deadlines
- Customer complaints
- Budget overruns
- Unexplained variances

## Intuitive Indicators

- "Something feels wrong" with a process
- Unusual patterns in behavior
- Gaps in communication
- Unspoken tensions in the team
