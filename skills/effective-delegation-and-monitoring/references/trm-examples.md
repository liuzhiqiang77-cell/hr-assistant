# Task-Relevant Maturity Examples

## High TRM in One Area, Low in Another
- Senior engineer moving to management role
- Product manager transitioning to new product category
- Sales representative handling enterprise vs. SMB accounts

## TRM Changes Over Time
- New hire: Low TRM → Needs close supervision
- After 6 months in role: Growing TRM → Less frequent check-ins
- After 2 years: High TRM → Minimal supervision

## Factors That Cause Low TRM
- New environment or company culture
- Different pace or intensity of work
- Abrupt job changes
- New tools or technologies
- Different stakeholder dynamics
