# Performance Diagnosis Examples

## Example 1: Sales Representative Not Meeting Quota

**Observation**: Sales rep consistently misses monthly targets

**Diagnosis Questions**:
- Do they know how to sell? (Capability)
- Do they have product knowledge? (Capability)
- Do they have access to leads? (Capability)
- Are they making enough calls? (Motivation)
- Do they seem discouraged? (Motivation)

**Scenario A - Can't do**: New rep lacks product knowledge
→ **Solution**: Training on product features, competitive positioning, sales techniques

**Scenario B - Won't do**: Experienced rep making few calls, seems burned out
→ **Solution**: Motivation through incentive restructuring, coaching, role clarification

## Example 2: Software Engineer Missing Deadlines

**Observation**: Engineer consistently delivers late

**Diagnosis Questions**:
- Do they understand the requirements? (Capability)
- Do they have the technical skills? (Capability)
- Are they overwhelmed by complexity? (Capability)
- Are they distracted by other priorities? (Motivation)
- Do they care about deadlines? (Motivation)

**Scenario A - Can't do**: Junior engineer struggling with complex architecture
→ **Solution**: Training, mentoring, pair programming, simplified initial tasks

**Scenario B - Won't do**: Senior engineer procrastinating, not communicating delays
→ **Solution**: Motivation through accountability, clear consequences, coaching on communication

## Example 3: Manager Not Developing Team

**Observation**: Team members are not growing, turnover is high

**Diagnosis Questions**:
- Does the manager know how to coach? (Capability)
- Do they have time for development? (Capability)
- Do they value team development? (Motivation)
- Are they focused only on short-term results? (Motivation)

**Scenario A - Can't do**: Promoted individual contributor never managed people
→ **Solution**: Management training, mentorship from experienced manager, clear development framework

**Scenario B - Won't do**: Manager believes "sink or swim" is best approach
→ **Solution**: Motivation through performance metrics that include team development, coaching on leadership philosophy
