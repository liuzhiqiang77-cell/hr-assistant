# Career Management: Daily Assessment Checklist

## Value Addition Audit
- [ ] Did I create something new today, or just process existing work?
- [ ] What specific improvement did I make to my team's output?
- [ ] Did my actions increase the value of what my organization produces?
- [ ] Can I point to a tangible result from today's work?

## Connectivity Check
- [ ] What new information did I learn about my industry today?
- [ ] Who did I connect with outside my immediate team?
- [ ] What trends am I tracking that could impact my role?
- [ ] Am I relying on others to interpret events for me?

## Innovation Review
- [ ] What new tool or technique did I try personally today?
- [ ] What did I learn from a failure or experiment?
- [ ] What skill am I currently developing?
- [ ] When was the last time I stepped outside my comfort zone?

## Competitive Position
- [ ] What makes me uniquely valuable compared to others globally?
- [ ] How am I staying current with changes in my field?
- [ ] What would happen if my role were eliminated tomorrow?
- [ ] What's my plan B if my current path becomes obsolete?
