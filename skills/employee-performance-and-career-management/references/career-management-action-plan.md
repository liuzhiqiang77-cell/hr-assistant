# Sole Proprietor Career Management: Action Plan

## Daily Practices

### Morning Routine
- [ ] Review today's tasks: "Which of these increase my value?"
- [ ] Identify one learning opportunity today
- [ ] Check industry news and trends

### During the Day
- [ ] Before each task: "Does this add real value?"
- [ ] Connect with at least one person outside immediate team
- [ ] Try one new approach or tool

### Evening Reflection
- [ ] What did I learn today?
- [ ] How did I increase my value?
- [ ] What new idea did I try?
- [ ] Who did I connect with?

## Weekly Review

### Value Addition Audit
- What tangible value did I create?
- What problems did I solve?
- What processes did I improve?

### Connectivity Check
- Who did I meet or talk to?
- What new information did I gather?
- Am I plugged into key networks?

### Innovation Assessment
- What new technique did I try?
- What new technology did I explore?
- What did I learn that I can apply?

## Monthly Strategic Review

### Competitive Position
- What are my top 3 strengths?
- What skills am I developing?
- Who are my competitors (globally)?
- How am I differentiating?

### Market Awareness
- What's changing in my industry?
- What skills are becoming more valuable?
- What skills are becoming obsolete?
- What opportunities am I missing?

### Career Path
- Am I growing or stagnating?
- Am I blocking others' growth?
- Should I move up, move on, or pivot?

## Annual Career Planning

### Skills Inventory
- Current skills and proficiency levels
- Skills to develop this year
- Skills to develop in 3-5 years

### Value Proposition
- What unique value do I provide?
- What problems can I solve that others can't?
- What's my competitive advantage?

### Network Mapping
- Key relationships in current organization
- Key relationships in industry
- Gaps in network
- Plan to build connections

### Scenario Planning
- If my current role disappeared, what would I do?
- If my industry changed dramatically, how would I adapt?
- What's my backup plan?

## Handling Slow/No-Growth Environments

### Self-Assessment
- Am I still adding significant value?
- Am I blocking ambitious juniors?
- Is there room for my growth here?

### Options
1. **Move Up**: Seek promotion to higher role
2. **Move On**: Find opportunity elsewhere
3. **Create Value**: Identify and solve new problems
4. **Mentor**: Help others grow (builds goodwill, may create opening)
