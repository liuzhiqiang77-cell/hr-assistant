# Black Box Assessment Examples

## Example 1: Sales Manager Assessment

**Context**: Annual performance review for sales manager

**Output Measures (70%)**:
- Team exceeded quota by 15%
- Revenue increased $2M
- Top performer closed 3 major deals

**Internal Measures (30%)**:
- Mentored 2 junior reps to quota achievement
- Implemented new CRM training program
- Maintained high team morale despite pressure

**Trade-off**: Slightly lower personal sales (output) offset by team development (internal)

## Example 2: Engineering Lead Assessment

**Context**: Quarterly review for technical lead

**Output Measures (40%)**:
- Delivered 2 major features on schedule
- Code quality metrics above average
- Resolved 15 critical bugs

**Internal Measures (60%)**:
- Architected scalable system for future growth
- Developed 3 senior engineers
- Created knowledge base reducing onboarding time
- Prevented potential technical debt crisis

**Trade-off**: Accepting slower delivery (output) for long-term architectural health (internal)

## Example 3: HR Director Assessment

**Context**: Annual review for HR director

**Output Measures (30%)**:
- Reduced turnover by 10%
- Filled 25 open positions
- Implemented new performance review system

**Internal Measures (70%)**:
- Built strong employer brand attracting top talent
- Developed succession plan for key roles
- Improved company culture scores
- Positioned HR as strategic partner to business
- Prevented potential legal exposure through policy updates

**Trade-off**: Focus on strategic positioning (internal) over operational metrics (output)

## Example 4: Crisis Manager Assessment

**Context**: Review during company crisis

**Output Measures (80%)**:
- Resolved critical customer issue within 48 hours
- Stabilized production line
- Communicated effectively with stakeholders

**Internal Measures (20%)**:
- Maintained team morale during stress
- Documented crisis response procedures
- Built relationships with key vendors for future

**Trade-off**: Prioritized immediate results (output) over long-term building (internal) due to crisis
