# Expanded Interview Question Bank

## Technical/Skills Assessment

### General
- "How good are you technically? Rate yourself on a scale of 1-10 and explain why."
- "What technical skills are you currently developing?"
- "Describe a technical problem you solved that others couldn't."

### Role-Specific Examples

**For Engineers**:
- "Walk me through how you would design a scalable system for X."
- "What's the most complex bug you've debugged? How did you approach it?"

**For Sales**:
- "Describe your most difficult sale. How did you close it?"
- "How do you handle objections?"

**For Managers**:
- "How do you handle underperforming team members?"
- "Describe a time you had to deliver difficult feedback."

---

## What They Did With Knowledge

### Achievements
- "What do you consider your most significant achievements? Why were they important to you?"
- "Describe a project where you had to learn something new quickly."

### Failures/Learning
- "Tell me about a significant failure. What did you learn?"
- "Describe a time you made a mistake. How did you handle it?"

---

## Discrepancies and Problem-Solving

### Current Problems
- "What are some of the problems you are encountering in your current position? How are you going about solving them? What could you have done to prevent them from cropping up?"
- "What frustrates you most about your current job?"

### Problem-Solving Approach
- "Describe your approach to solving complex problems."
- "How do you make decisions when you don't have all the information?"

---

## Operational Values and Fit

### Readiness
- "Why do you think you're ready for this new job?"
- "What makes you think you'd be successful here?"

### Motivation
- "Convince me why my company should hire you."
- "Why do you want to leave your current position?"
