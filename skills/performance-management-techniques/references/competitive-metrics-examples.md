# Competitive Metrics - Implementation Examples

## Facility Management Example

**Scenario:** Multiple buildings need maintenance improvement

**Implementation:**
- Assign "building czar" to each facility
- Create scoring rubric for maintenance quality
- Publish scores monthly across all buildings
- No monetary rewards - just competition

**Result:** Immediate improvement without additional cost

---

## Sales Team Example

**Scenario:** Sales team needs motivation boost

**Implementation:**
- Leaderboard showing closed deals by rep
- Weekly "top performer" recognition
- Conversion rate rankings
- Activity metrics (calls, meetings) comparison

**Rules:**
- All metrics visible to entire team
- Rankings updated daily/weekly
- Coach provides feedback on improvement areas

---

## Software Development Example

**Scenario:** Development teams need velocity improvement

**Implementation:**
- Story points completed per sprint
- Bug count comparison
- Code review participation rates
- Deployment frequency metrics

**Rules:**
- Teams compete against each other
- Metrics tracked on public dashboard
- Retrospectives focus on improving scores

---

## Customer Service Example

**Scenario:** Support team needs quality improvement

**Implementation:**
- Customer satisfaction scores per agent
- Average resolution time rankings
- First-contact resolution rates
- Ticket volume handled

**Rules:**
- Individual and team scores
- Monthly "service champion" recognition
- Coach provides specific feedback on low scores

---

## Key Principles for Effective Metrics

1. **Visibility:** All participants can see scores
2. **Timeliness:** Updates happen frequently (daily/weekly)
3. **Clarity:** What's being measured is unambiguous
4. **Fairness:** Same rules apply to everyone
5. **Actionability:** Participants can influence their scores

## Pitfalls to Avoid

- Metrics that encourage gaming the system
- Competition that becomes destructive
- Metrics outside individual's control
- Over-emphasis on one metric at expense of others
- Public shaming of low performers
