# Performance Diagnosis Examples

## Example 1: Missed Deadlines

**Situation**: Employee consistently misses project deadlines despite knowing the timeline.

**Diagnosis**:
- Ask: Does the employee know HOW to manage time and prioritize?
- Ask: Does the employee understand the importance of deadlines?

**If Can't Do**: Time management training, project management tools, prioritization coaching

**If Won't Do**: Conversation about consequences, clarity on why deadlines matter, check for competing priorities

## Example 2: Poor Quality Work

**Situation**: Employee's work contains errors and doesn't meet quality standards.

**Diagnosis**:
- Ask: Has the employee been trained on quality standards?
- Ask: Does the employee have the technical skills to produce quality work?

**If Can't Do**: Training on quality processes, skills development, pairing with high performer, clear quality checklists

**If Won't Do**: Emphasize impact of poor quality, connect quality to customer satisfaction, review consequences

## Example 3: Not Taking Initiative

**Situation**: Employee does exactly what they're told and nothing more.

**Diagnosis**:
- Ask: Does the employee know WHAT initiatives would be valuable?
- Ask: Has the employee been given permission and encouragement to take initiative?

**If Can't Do**: Training on identifying improvement opportunities, coaching on problem identification, examples of desired initiative

**If Won't Do**: Reward initiative when shown, clarify that initiative is expected, discuss career growth tied to initiative

## Example 4: Conflict with Team Members

**Situation**: Employee frequently argues with colleagues and creates tension.

**Diagnosis**:
- Ask: Does the employee know HOW to communicate constructively?
- Ask: Does the employee understand the impact of their behavior?

**If Can't Do**: Communication skills training, conflict resolution training, coaching on specific situations, role-playing

**If Won't Do**: Clear feedback on behavior impact, discussion of values and team expectations, consequences if behavior continues

# Diagnostic Questions

## Assessing Capability (Can't Do)
- Have you received training on this task/skill?
- Do you understand the process completely?
- Do you have the tools and resources you need?
- Have you done this type of work successfully before?
- What would help you perform better?
- Can you demonstrate the task for me?
- What part of the task is most difficult for you?

## Assessing Motivation (Won't Do)
- Do you understand why this task is important?
- How does this task contribute to our goals?
- What would make this work more meaningful to you?
- Is there something preventing you from wanting to do this?
- What would motivate you to perform better?
- Do you feel recognized for your good work?
- Is there a mismatch between this work and your interests?
