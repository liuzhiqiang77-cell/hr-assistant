# Linearity Indicator Example

## Recruiting Campaign

**Target**: 100 new hires by June 30
**Timeline**: 6 months (Jan-Jun)
**Ideal Line**: 16.7 hires per month

## Progress Tracking

| Month | Cumulative Hires | Ideal Line | Variance | Required Rate |
|-------|------------------|------------|----------|---------------|
| Jan | 15 | 17 | -2 | 17.0 |
| Feb | 30 | 33 | -3 | 17.5 |
| Mar | 42 | 50 | -8 | 19.3 |
| Apr | 55 | 67 | -12 | 22.5 |
| May | 75 | 83 | -8 | 25.0 |
| Jun | 100 | 100 | 0 | - |

## Analysis

**April Situation**:
- Actual: 55 hires
- Ideal: 67 hires
- Variance: -12 hires
- Remaining months: 2 (May, Jun)
- Remaining target: 45 hires
- Required rate: 22.5 hires/month (vs. 16.7 ideal)

**Action Required**:
- Increase recruiting efforts immediately
- Add recruiters or sourcing channels
- Accelerate interview process
- Consider signing bonuses

**Result**: By increasing rate to 25 in May and 25 in June, target achieved.
