# Stagger Chart Template

## Structure

| Forecast Made | Jan | Feb | Mar | Apr | May | Jun |
|--------------|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| July | 100 | 110 | 120 | 130 | 140 | 150 |
| August | - | 105 | 115 | 125 | 135 | 145 |
| September | - | - | 118 | 128 | 138 | 148 |
| October | - | - | - | 125 | 135 | 145 |

## Analysis

### December Forecast Evolution
- July forecast for December: 150
- August forecast for December: 145
- September forecast for December: 148
- October forecast for December: 145

**Interpretation**: Outlook deteriorated from July to August, improved slightly in September, then stabilized. Indicates changing business conditions.

### Benefits
1. Shows evolution of expectations over time
2. Forces forecaster accountability (past forecasts visible)
3. More valuable than simple trend chart
4. Reveals business trend changes early
