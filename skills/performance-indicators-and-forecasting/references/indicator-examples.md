# Indicator Design Examples

## Manufacturing

**Primary Pair:**
- Units produced per hour
- Defect rate per 100 units

**Secondary Pair:**
- On-time delivery rate
- Customer return rate

**Operational Pair:**
- Equipment uptime %
- Maintenance cost per unit

## Sales

**Primary Pair:**
- Orders obtained
- Average order value

**Efficiency Pair:**
- Calls per hour
- Conversion rate %

**Quality Pair:**
- Customer satisfaction score
- Repeat purchase rate

## Administrative Functions

### Accounts Payable
- Vouchers processed per day
- Errors found per 100 vouchers
- Payment timeliness %

### Human Resources
- Positions filled per month
- Time-to-fill average
- New hire retention rate (90 days)

### IT Support
- Tickets resolved per day
- First-contact resolution %
- Customer satisfaction rating

### Customer Service
- Calls answered per hour
- Average handle time
- First-call resolution %

## Linearity Indicator Examples

### Production
- **Target**: 10,000 units by month-end
- **Day 15**: Should be at 5,000 (linear)
- **Actual**: 3,000 units
- **Action**: Increase production rate to 700/day (from 333/day average)

### Sales
- **Target**: $1M by quarter-end
- **Month 2**: Should be at $667K (linear)
- **Actual**: $400K
- **Action**: Intensify sales efforts, promotional campaigns

### Hiring
- **Target**: 50 new hires by year-end
- **October**: Should be at 42 hires (linear)
- **Actual**: 30 hires
- **Action**: Increase recruiting resources, expand candidate sources
