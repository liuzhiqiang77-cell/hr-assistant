# Andrew S. Grove - Professional Background

## Career Timeline
- **1956**: Emigrated from Hungary to the United States
- **Intel Founding**: Participated in the founding of Intel Corporation
- **1979**: Became President of Intel
- **1987**: Became Chief Executive Officer of Intel
- **1997**: Named Time magazine's Man of the Year
- **1998**: Retired as CEO
- **2004**: Retired as Chairman
- **Academic**: Taught at Stanford University Graduate School of Business for twenty-four years

## Key Insight
Grove explicitly states: "I learned everything I know about how to manage from them" (referring to Intel managers).
