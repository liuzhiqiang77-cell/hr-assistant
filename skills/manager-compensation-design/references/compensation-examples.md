## Compensation Design Examples

### Example 1: Middle Manager Compensation Package

**Profile**:
- Role: Engineering Manager
- Total Target Compensation: $150,000
- Experience: 8 years

**Design**:
- **Base Salary**: $127,500 (85% of total)
- **Target Bonus**: $22,500 (15% of total)

**Bonus Split**:
- Individual Performance (50%): $11,250 - Based on supervisor review
- Team Performance (30%): $6,750 - Based on department OKRs
- Corporate Performance (20%): $4,500 - Based on company revenue/profit

**Salary Curve**:
- Starts at entry level: $100,000
- With 8 years experience and "Exceeds" ratings: $127,500
- Merit variance: +$15,000 above experience curve

### Example 2: Senior Manager Compensation Package

**Profile**:
- Role: Director of Engineering
- Total Target Compensation: $300,000
- Experience: 15 years

**Design**:
- **Base Salary**: $210,000 (70% of total)
- **Target Bonus**: $90,000 (30% of total)

**Bonus Split**:
- Individual Performance (30%): $27,000 - Based on executive review
- Division Performance (40%): $36,000 - Based on division P&L
- Corporate Financial Performance (30%): $27,000 - Based on company EPS/ROIC

**Salary Curve**:
- Starts at entry level: $180,000
- With 15 years experience and "Exceeds" ratings: $210,000
- Merit variance: +$25,000 above experience curve

### Example 3: Bonus Payout Scenarios

**Scenario**: Middle Manager with $22,500 target bonus

**Year 1**:
- Individual Rating: Exceeds (110% of target)
- Team Performance: Missed objectives (70% of target)
- Corporate Performance: Exceeded (110% of target)
- Calculation: $11,250×1.1 + $6,750×0.7 + $4,500×1.1 = $12,375 + $4,725 + $4,950 = $22,050
- **Payout**: $22,050 (98% of target)

**Year 2**:
- Individual Rating: Meets (100% of target)
- Team Performance: Exceeded (120% of target)
- Corporate Performance: Met (100% of target)
- Calculation: $11,250×1.0 + $6,750×1.2 + $4,500×1.0 = $11,250 + $8,100 + $4,500 = $23,850
- **Payout**: $23,850 (106% of target)
