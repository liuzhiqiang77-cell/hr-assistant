# Manager Compensation Design - Detailed Reference

## Variable Definitions

- **total_compensation** (Currency): The total annual compensation of the manager
- **bonus_percentage_target** (Percentage): Target % of total comp allocated to bonus (10-25% for middle, up to 50% for senior)
- **performance_rating** (Score/Subjective): Supervisor's judgment of individual performance
- **team_metric** (Objective/KPI): Measurable performance of the immediate team or department
- **corporate_financial_result** (Currency/Percentage): Overall financial performance of the company

## Result Template
A compensation plan consisting of a Base Salary (determined by experience/merit curve) and a Performance Bonus (determined by level and multi-factor metrics).
