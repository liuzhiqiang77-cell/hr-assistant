# Bonus Calculation Examples

## Example 1: Middle Manager - 3-Factor Split

**Profile:**
- Total Compensation: $120,000
- Bonus Target: 20% of total comp
- Bonus Pool: $24,000

**Factor Weights:**
- Individual Performance: 50% ($12,000)
- Team Performance: 30% ($7,200)
- Corporate Performance: 20% ($4,800)

**Performance Results:**
- Individual Rating: "Exceeds Expectations" (120% of target)
- Team Metric: Revenue achieved at 95% of target
- Corporate Result: Net income achieved at 110% of target

**Calculation:**
```
Individual Bonus: $12,000 × 1.20 = $14,400
Team Bonus: $7,200 × 0.95 = $6,840
Corporate Bonus: $4,800 × 1.10 = $5,280

Total Bonus: $14,400 + $6,840 + $5,280 = $26,520
```

**Effective Bonus Percentage:** 22.1% of total compensation

## Example 2: Senior Manager - Higher Bonus Percentage

**Profile:**
- Total Compensation: $250,000
- Bonus Target: 40% of total comp
- Bonus Pool: $100,000

**Factor Weights:**
- Individual Performance: 40% ($40,000)
- Division Performance: 40% ($40,000)
- Corporate Performance: 20% ($20,000)

**Performance Results:**
- Individual Rating: "Meets Expectations" (100% of target)
- Division Metric: EBITDA achieved at 105% of target
- Corporate Result: Stock price up 15% (target was 10%)

**Calculation:**
```
Individual Bonus: $40,000 × 1.00 = $40,000
Division Bonus: $40,000 × 1.05 = $42,000
Corporate Bonus: $20,000 × 1.50 = $30,000

Total Bonus: $40,000 + $42,000 + $30,000 = $112,000
```

**Effective Bonus Percentage:** 44.8% of total compensation

## Example 3: Middle Manager - Poor Performance

**Profile:**
- Total Compensation: $100,000
- Bonus Target: 15% of total comp
- Bonus Pool: $15,000

**Factor Weights:**
- Individual Performance: 60% ($9,000)
- Team Performance: 40% ($6,000)

**Performance Results:**
- Individual Rating: "Below Expectations" (70% of target)
- Team Metric: Customer satisfaction at 80% of target

**Calculation:**
```
Individual Bonus: $9,000 × 0.70 = $6,300
Team Bonus: $6,000 × 0.80 = $4,800

Total Bonus: $6,300 + $4,800 = $11,100
```

**Effective Bonus Percentage:** 11.1% of total compensation

## Bonus Cap and Floor Considerations

**Common Practices:**
- **Floor:** 0% (no bonus for poor performance)
- **Cap:** 150% of target (exceptional performance)

**Example with Cap:**
- Target Bonus: $20,000
- Calculated Bonus: $35,000 (175% of target)
- Capped Bonus: $30,000 (150% of target)
