# Detailed Question Categories

## Technical/Skills Questions
- "Describe some projects that were highly regarded by your management..."
- "What are your weaknesses? How are you working to eliminate them?"

## Knowledge Application Questions
- "What do you consider your most significant achievements? Why were they important to you?"
- "What are some of the problems you are encountering... How are you going about solving them? What could you have done to prevent them?"

## Operational Values Questions
- "Convince me why my company should hire you."
- "Why do you think you're ready for this new job?"

## Direct Question Technique
Blunt questions to elicit direct answers and observe confidence levels.

## Hypothetical Scenario Examples
- Manufacturing process walkthrough
- Cost calculation exercises (e.g., finished cost of a wafer)
- Problem-solving in real-time situations
