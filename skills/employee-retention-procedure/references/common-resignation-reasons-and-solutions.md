# Common Resignation Reasons and Solutions

## Feeling Unappreciated

**Symptoms:**
- Hard work goes unrecognized
- No feedback on performance
- Achievements are ignored
- Manager takes credit for subordinate's work

**Solutions:**
- Implement regular recognition program
- Public acknowledgment of achievements
- Manager training on giving feedback
- Create visibility for subordinate's contributions
- Regular performance reviews with specific praise

## Lack of Growth Opportunities

**Symptoms:**
- Stuck in same role for extended time
- No clear career path
- Training not offered
- New skills not being developed

**Solutions:**
- Create individual development plan
- Identify stretch assignments
- Offer training opportunities
- Discuss career path explicitly
- Consider promotion or lateral move

## Poor Management

**Symptoms:**
- Micromanagement
- Lack of support
- Inconsistent direction
- Unreasonable expectations

**Solutions:**
- Manager coaching or training
- Transfer to different department
- Clarify expectations and boundaries
- Regular check-ins to adjust approach
- Consider reporting line change

## Work-Life Balance Issues

**Symptoms:**
- Excessive overtime
- No flexibility
- Burnout
- Personal life suffering

**Solutions:**
- Adjust workload or priorities
- Offer flexible schedule
- Provide additional resources
- Review and adjust deadlines
- Encourage time off

## Lack of Autonomy

**Symptoms:**
- Every decision needs approval
- No ownership of work
- Ideas dismissed
- Treated like child, not professional

**Solutions:**
- Delegate decision-making authority
- Set clear boundaries of autonomy
- Involve in planning and strategy
- Trust and verify approach
- Recognize independent thinking

## Toxic Team Culture

**Symptoms:**
- Conflict with colleagues
- Gossip and politics
- Lack of collaboration
- Hostile environment

**Solutions:**
- Transfer to different team
- Address toxic behavior directly
- Team building interventions
- Mediation between parties
- Culture change initiatives

## Compensation (When It's Not Just About Money)

**Symptoms:**
- Paid below market
- No raises in years
- Peers paid more for similar work
- Financial stress affecting performance

**Solutions:**
- Market salary adjustment
- Performance-based bonus
- Review compensation structure
- Non-monetary compensation (perks, benefits)
- Clear path to future increases

**Note:** If compensation is the ONLY issue and the employee has a significantly better offer, this procedure may not be effective.
