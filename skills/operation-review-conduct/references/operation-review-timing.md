# Operation Review Timing Guidelines

## Rule of Thumb
**4 minutes per visual aid**

## What Counts as a Visual Aid
- Overhead transparencies
- Tables
- Numbers/charts
- Graphics
- Slides

## Time Allocation Breakdown
Per visual aid:
- ~2 minutes presentation
- ~2 minutes discussion/questions

## Examples
| Visual Aids | Total Time |
|-------------|------------|
| 5 | 20 minutes |
| 10 | 40 minutes |
| 15 | 60 minutes |

## Why This Matters
- Ensures presenters don't rush through material
- Guarantees time for audience questions
- Prevents presentations from running over
- Allows for meaningful interaction between levels
