# Motivation Diagnosis Scenarios

## Scenario 1: The Goal-Quitter

**Situation:** Factory worker requests overtime to save $10,000 for a down payment. After reaching the goal, quits despite offer of more overtime.

**Diagnosis:**
- Focus: Absolute sum ($10,000 target)
- Mode: Physiological/Safety (deficit need)
- Money as: Utilitarian resource

**Management Response:**
- Understand the goal was met
- Offer new goals or different motivators
- Don't expect more money to motivate without new target

---

## Scenario 2: The Comparison-Seeker

**Situation:** Manager receives $20,000 raise but is unhappy because peer received $25,000.

**Diagnosis:**
- Focus: Relative comparison
- Mode: Esteem/Recognition
- Money as: Scorecard for achievement

**Management Response:**
- Address fairness concerns
- Provide recognition and status symbols
- Set achievement-based goals
- Money alone won't satisfy without recognition component

---

## Scenario 3: The Boundless Achiever

**Situation:** CEO already worth $100M works 80-hour weeks to grow company to $1B valuation.

**Diagnosis:**
- Focus: Achievement and growth
- Mode: Self-Actualization
- Money as: Measure of achievement

**Management Response:**
- Provide challenging goals at capability boundary
- Create competitive environment
- Emphasize output and results
- Money is secondary to achievement recognition

---

## Scenario 4: The Security-Seeker

**Situation:** Employee turns down higher-paying startup job for stable corporate role with pension.

**Diagnosis:**
- Focus: Protection against loss
- Mode: Safety/Security
- Money as: Security resource

**Management Response:**
- Emphasize stability and benefits
- Provide long-term security guarantees
- Avoid high-risk assignments
- Highlight insurance and retirement benefits

---

## Scenario 5: The Social Belonger

**Situation:** Employee takes pay cut to join team of friends at another company.

**Diagnosis:**
- Focus: Companionship and belonging
- Mode: Social/Affiliation
- Money as: Secondary to social connection

**Management Response:**
- Foster team identity and culture
- Create social opportunities
- Emphasize team achievements
- Money alone won't retain without social element
