# Black Box Assessment Examples

## Example 1: The High Performer with Team Issues

**Output Measures (Weight: 60%):**
- Exceeded sales quota by 40%: 9/10
- Closed 3 major deals: 9/10
- Revenue generated: $2.5M: 10/10

**Internal Measures (Weight: 40%):**
- Team collaboration: 3/10 (hoards information)
- Mentoring juniors: 2/10 (refuses to help)
- Process improvement: 5/10 (resists changes)

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- High individual output vs negative team impact
- Short-term revenue gain vs long-term team capability

**Overall Assessment:** Good performer with coaching needed on collaboration. Bonus awarded but with development plan.

## Example 2: The Builder

**Output Measures (Weight: 30%):**
- Projects delivered: 6/10 (slightly behind schedule)
- Features completed: 7/10 (good quality, slower pace)
- Bugs fixed: 6/10 (average)

**Internal Measures (Weight: 70%):**
- Team development: 9/10 (mentored 3 juniors to promotion)
- Documentation: 10/10 (comprehensive knowledge base)
- Process improvement: 9/10 (implemented CI/CD pipeline)
- Department health: 8/10 (improved morale)

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- Lower current output vs significant future capability building
- Individual productivity vs team multiplier effect

**Overall Assessment:** Excellent strategic contributor. Promotion recommended despite modest current output.

## Example 3: Crisis Response

**Output Measures (Weight: 90%):**
- Critical system restored: 10/10 (under 1 hour)
- Customer issues resolved: 9/10 (95% satisfaction)
- Revenue protection: 10/10 (saved $500K)

**Internal Measures (Weight: 10%):**
- Team communication: 5/10 (focused, not collaborative)
- Documentation: 3/10 (no time to document)
- Future planning: 2/10 (crisis mode)

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- Crisis response vs normal operations
- Short-term survival vs long-term maintenance

**Overall Assessment:** Outstanding crisis performance. Bonus awarded, expectation to address documentation post-crisis.

## Example 4: The Developer in Transition

**Output Measures (Weight: 40%):**
- Code delivered: 7/10 (good quality, slower pace)
- Features completed: 6/10 (learning new stack)
- Bugs: 5/10 (expected with new technology)

**Internal Measures (Weight: 60%):**
- Skill acquisition: 10/10 (mastered new framework in 2 months)
- Knowledge sharing: 9/10 (training team on new tech)
- Future readiness: 10/10 (positioning team for migration)

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- Current productivity vs strategic technology transition
- Individual output vs organizational capability upgrade

**Overall Assessment:** Strong strategic contributor. Performance rated as "exceeds expectations" given transition context.

## Weighting Guidelines by Role

### Individual Contributors
- **Output:** 60-70%
- **Internal:** 30-40%
- Focus: Deliverables and personal development

### Team Leads
- **Output:** 50-60%
- **Internal:** 40-50%
- Focus: Team results and team development

### Managers
- **Output:** 40-50%
- **Internal:** 50-60%
- Focus: Organizational outcomes and capability building

### Senior Leaders
- **Output:** 30-40%
- **Internal:** 60-70%
- Focus: Strategic results and organizational health
