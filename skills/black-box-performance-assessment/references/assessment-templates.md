## Trade-off Analysis Framework

### Step 1: Identify the Trade-off
Document which measures are in conflict:
- High output / Low internal contribution
- Low output / High internal contribution
- Strong current results / Weak future positioning

### Step 2: Assess Context
Consider:
- Employee's role and level
- Organizational priorities
- Time period circumstances
- Team dynamics

### Step 3: Determine Rationale
Document why the trade-off is acceptable:
- "Critical deadline required focus"
- "Investment phase for future returns"
- "External factors impacted output"
- "Strategic pivot in progress"

### Step 4: Set Future Focus
Establish what will change:
- "Next quarter: rebalance to internal measures"
- "Address specific gap: improve documentation"
- "Maintain strength: continue high output"
