# Daily Operations Review

## When to Use
- Starting daily operations
- Arriving at the office
- Managing production or service delivery systems
- Ensuring operational readiness

## Procedure

### 1. Review Sales Forecast
- Determine number of units planned for delivery today

### 2. Analyze Variance
- Compare yesterday's actual deliveries vs. planned deliveries
- Assess confidence in today's forecast based on variance

### 3. Check Raw Material Inventory
- Verify stock levels of essential items (eggs, bread, coffee, etc.)
- **IF inventory too low**: Order more supplies immediately
- **IF inventory too high**: Cancel scheduled deliveries

### 4. Assess Equipment Condition
- Check if any equipment broke down yesterday
- **IF breakdown occurred**:
  - Arrange repairs immediately
  - Rearrange production line to meet forecast

### 5. Evaluate Manpower
- Check staff availability (sick leave, absences)
- **IF understaffed**:
  - Call in temporary help, OR
  - Reallocate staff from other lines

### 6. Monitor Quality Indicator
- Review customer complaint log
- **IF staff member elicited excessive complaints**:
  - Speak to that staff member immediately
- Address quality issues proactively
